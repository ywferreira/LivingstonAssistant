# Speakers Bureau

## Requests

The Livingston Police Department has officers who can speak on a variety of topics to audiences from children through adults. These topics include bullying prevention, cyber safety, personal safety, home security, bicycle safety, fraud, and many others.

If your group would like an officer to come speak on a topic related to law enforcement, please fill out the [online request form](/FormCenter/Police-6/Police-Officer-Speaker-Request-49) and someone will get in touch with you.
