# Community Planning Calendar: Burbio

## A Comprehensive Community Calendar

The [Burbio Community Planning Calendar](https://www.burbio.com/lists/livingston-community-planning-calendar/calendar) pulls all the events and programs for all calendars from all Township groups.

### Ideal for Scheduling

The Burbio Community Planning Calendar is a great calendar to check if you're trying to choose a date or time for an event or program and want to be sure it doesn't conflict with any others in town.

The Community Planning Calendar feeds from the online calendars created and populated by the schools, Township website, committees, sports, and other organizations.

**Please keep in mind:** Because some events or programs are on multiple different calendars, they are pulled through several different feeds and therefore may show up multiple times on the same day on this Community Planning Calendar.
