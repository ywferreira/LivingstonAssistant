# Golf Tournament Committee

The Township of Livingston has been sponsoring a golf outing for over 50 years. The purpose of the outing is to bring residents together in a fun environment and provide the opportunity to meet new people. More recently, the golf outing has also become a fundraiser to support our township volunteer programs, such as Livingston Neighbors Helping Neighbors and the Livingston Volunteer First Aid Squad.

This past year the event was held at the Cedar Hill Country Club in Livingston. There were nearly 100 participants and funds were raised for the Livingston Volunteer First Aid Squad. The event was sponsored by Somerset Regal Bank.

Members of the committee work to organize the event, including inviting participants, soliciting sponsors, and managing the event.
