# News Flash

# Welcome to Recycle Coach!

Garbage and Recycling

Posted on March 15, 2024

Much more than just a calendar, Recycle Coach offers innovative technology that inspires positive change in recycling behavior. The app empowers residents and puts vital information in their hands via an interactive desktop or mobile experience and improves recycling confidence through engaging features, recycling tips, and a comprehensive "What goes where?” search tool.

Livingston is pleased to now be part of the Recycle Coach network. Residents can discover local garbage and recycling disposal information for thousands of household items for free. Through Recycle Coach, Livingston will continue its commitment to educating residents and building better recycling habits.

With Recycle Coach, residents can:

* Find the closest recycling and disposal facility for household items based on their zip code
* Become recycling experts with the “What goes where?” search tool, complete with local garbage and recycling disposal information, as well as collection requirements for common and unique household items
* Test their knowledge with weekly ‘We Wanted to Know’ quizzes that leverage local data to increase recycling confidence
* Stay organized with a customized curbside collection and events calendar
* Receive collection reminders and real-time updates on service disruptions
* Use the problem reporting tool, equipped with geolocation, to notify municipal customer service representatives about issues like a missed collection

Recycle Coach takes the guesswork out of recycling, enabling residents to play an active role in reducing household waste and make smarter recycling decisions. For Livingston, this can translate into higher recycling rates and less contamination in the waste stream – a win-win for everyone.

Residents are encouraged to become better recyclers by downloading Recycle Coach for free in the [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/recycle-coach/id1071714967) or [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=mobi.recyclecoach.worldster.pack&pli=1).

## More Information

Residents can access Recycle Coach at [livingstonnj.org/GarbageRecycling](https://livingstonnj.org/287/Garbage-Recycling) or through the app in the [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/recycle-coach/id1071714967) or [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=mobi.recyclecoach.worldster.pack&pli=1).

For more information about Recycle Coach, please visit [recyclecoach.com](https://recyclecoach.com/).
