# Electronics Recycling: Items Accepted

## Why do I have to recycle electronics?

By law, electronic items must be recycled separately and cannot be put out and collected with regular garbage.

## Items Accepted for Electronics Recycling

Items accepted at Livingston's Electronic Recycling Events include:

* Cellular Telephones (erase memory if possible)
* Computers & Components (erase or remove hard drives if possible)
* Copiers
* DVD Players
* Electronic Games
* Fax Machines
* Keyboards
* Laptops
* Monitors/Flat Screens
* Pagers
* PDAs
* Portable Electronic Music Devices
* Printers, including 3D Printers
* Radios
* Scanners
* Stereos
* Telecom Equipment
* Televisions
* USB and Other Cables
* VCRs

### Other Special Items

* Fluorescent bulbs: Fluorescent light bulbs of all shapes, up to 4 feet long, will be collected. There is a limit of 6 bulbs per resident. NO broken bulbs. To comply with hazardous materials handling, there is no early drop allowed off for bulbs.
* Rechargeable batteries: These collection events now include drop-off of rechargeable lithium, NiCad, sealed lead-acid (rectangular alarm or exit light types) and power tool batteries. Volume is limited. Early drop-off is prohibited, as it creates a serious fire hazard. Rechargeable batteries can also be dropped off at an [Essex County Household Hazardous Waste Disposal Event](https://www.ecuanj.com/). (Note: Ordinary alkaline batteries should go in your household garbage.)

## Items Not Accepted for Electronics Recycling

These items **are not** accepted at Livingston's Electronics Recycling Events:

* Air Conditioners: [Register for Metal Pickup](https://livingstonnj.org/308/Metal-Collection)
* Audio and Video Tapes: Put in regular garbage for pickup
* CDs and DVDs: Put in regular garbage for pickup
* Dehumidifiers: [Register for Metal Pickup](https://livingstonnj.org/308/Metal-Collection)
* Diskettes: Put in regular garbage for pickup
* Ink Cartridges for Inkjet/Laser Printers and Fax Machines: Some retailers offer store credit or money back for returned empty ink cartridges; check with local retailers
* Irons: [Register for Metal Pickup](https://livingstonnj.org/308/Metal-Collection)
* Microwave Ovens: [Register for Metal Pickup](https://livingstonnj.org/308/Metal-Collection)
* Smoke Detectors: Put in regular garbage for pickup (only 1 per pickup)
* Speakers: Put in regular garbage for pickup
* Toasters and Toaster Ovens: [Register for Metal Pickup](https://livingstonnj.org/308/Metal-Collection)
* Vacuum Cleaners: Put in regular garbage for pickup
* White Goods (Large Appliances -- e.g., Washers, Dryers, Refrigerators): [Register for Metal Pickup](https://livingstonnj.org/308/Metal-Collection)
* Yard Appliances: [Register for Metal Pickup](https://livingstonnj.org/308/Metal-Collection)
* Zip Disks: Put in regular garbage for pickup
