### Council Meeting Times

* 6:30 p.m. Conference Meetings
* 7:30 p.m. Regular Meetings
* Conference Meetings may continue at the conclusion of Regular Meetings

### Agendas & Minutes

Agendas are available prior to the meetings. Minutes are available following approval.

View the most recent [Agendas and Minutes here](/129/Agendas-Minutes) or by clicking on the "Agenda and Minutes" icon on the homepage.

### Council Meeting Dates

[View the Township Council Calendar](/calendar.aspx?CID=35) for the schedule of upcoming meetings.

View a [printable Township Council Meeting Schedule (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/33653/2026-Council-Meeting-Schedule-PDF). However, remember to also check the meeting calendar on the website, as meeting dates and times may change throughout the year.

### Watch Council Meetings

Township Council Meetings are live streamed on our [Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/LivingstonTownshipNJ) and can be viewed there in real time.

Find recorded Township Council Meeting videos on our [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@livingstontownshipnj).

[Livingston Television (LTV)](/485/Livingston-Television-LTV) broadcasts Town Council meetings on Thursdays and Sundays.
