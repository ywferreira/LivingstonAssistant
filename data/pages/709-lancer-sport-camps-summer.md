\*\*Camp is held on the courts at the Memorial Oval.

Individual sport camps are conducted by the Livingston High School coaching staff. All camps are specifically “geared” towards providing each participant with the ingredients for an action-packed learning and fun camp experience. Participants will have hands-on training and be supervised by the LHS Coaching Staff in each sport.

Camp participants are brought to the Mervyn V.T. Haines Community Pool from 1:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. to make camp a full-day experience. Pool membership not required. Campers must know how to swim. Parents must pick up campers at the pool no later than 5:00 p.m. Parents will be charged $10.00 every 15 minutes after 5:00 p.m.
