# Blue Star

## Assistance Program

Blue Star is an assistance program that allows a senior citizen or handicapped person living alone to register their information with the Police Department.

As part of the program, a house key is left at Police Headquarters and kept in a secure location so that access to the person’s residence can be gained quickly in case of an emergency. A separate key may also be left with the individual’s neighbor, if desired.

Please complete the [Blue Star Form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/27880). The completed form and a house key can be dropped off at Police Headquarters, 333 S. Livingston Avenue, 24 hours a day.
