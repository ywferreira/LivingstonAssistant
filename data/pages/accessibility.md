# Website Accessibility

If you use assistive technology (such as a Braille reader, or a screen reader) and the format of any material on this website interferes with your ability to access information, please [contact us](/contactus). To enable us to respond in a manner most helpful to you, please indicate the nature of your accessibility problem, the preferred format in which to receive the material, the web address of the requested material, and your contact information.

## Accessibility Design Guidelines

Our website has been designed with the following accessibility guidelines in mind:

* We work to comply with [Section 508](https://www.section508.gov/) as well as [WCAG 2.0 A and AA](https://www.w3.org/WAI/intro/wcag) referring to website accessibility standards.
* If you have difficulty accessing the site or have any comments or feedback, please do not hesitate to [contact us](/contactus).

## Additional Plug-ins

Adobe Reader is required to view and print PDF documents that appear on this website.

* To download this program for free, visit the [Adobe website](https://www.adobe.com/products/reader.html).
* To read PDF documents with a screen reader, please visit the [Adobe Reader Accessibility website](https://helpx.adobe.com/reader/using/accessibility-features.html) which provides useful tools and resources.
