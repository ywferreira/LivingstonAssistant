# Hazardous Materials

Essex County holds free household hazardous waste collection days. Specific information as to date, location, and accepted materials can be found at [Essex County Utility Authority](http://www.ecuanj.com/index.htm).

Questions? Call the Essex County Utilities Authority at 973-857-2350.
