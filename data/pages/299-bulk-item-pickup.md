# Bulk Item Pickup

Bulk garbage collection occurs once per month, on your last regular garbage pickup day.

Reminder: Bulk pickup is based on the date, not on the day of the week.

Each household is limited to **five** bulky items for each pickup. The weight of all items must not exceed a total of 150 pounds. Items must be able to fit in a garbage truck.

## Bulk Items Include:

* Bookcases
* Branches three to four inches in diameter in bundles (not to exceed four feet in length)
* Carpets and rugs rolled and tied into four-foot lengths, weighing no more than 50 pounds
* Chairs
* Couches
* Doors
* Garden and lawn trimmings in bags
* Glass & mirrors -- tabletops, shower doors and other glass doors, etc.
  + **NOTE:** These require special handling prior to disposal.**Always duct tape a large "X"**across the front and back of glass and mirrored items to avoid breaking/shattering.
* Mattresses and box springs
* Rimless automobile tires
* Screens
* Sleeper sofas
* Tables
* Toilets
* Toys

## Appliances / Scrap Metal

The following items are [Appliances / Scrap Metal](/308/Metal-Collection) and must be disposed of separately. **Do not put them out with bulk:**

* Air conditioners
* Auto parts
* Desks
* Dryers
* Freezers
* Refrigerators
* Washers

## Electronics

The following items are [Electronics](/303/Electronics-Recycling-Events) and must be disposed of separately. **Do not put them out with bulk:**

* Cell phones and telephone systems
* Computer hard drives
* Computer monitors
* Computer peripherals such as USBs and other cables
* Electronic circuit boards and components
* Electronic games
* Fax machines and central office equipment
* Laptops
* Mainframes

* Monitors and flat screens
* Portable electronic music devices and PDAs
* Printers and copiers
* Rechargeable batteries
* Scanners
* Stereo equipment
* Telephones
* TVs
