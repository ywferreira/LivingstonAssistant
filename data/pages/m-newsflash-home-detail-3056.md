# 2026 Livingston Summer Aquatics Program Guide

Recreation & Senior Services

Posted on March 11, 2026

Dive into summer at the Community Pool! Whether you’re joining the spirited Aquajets swim team, taking swim lessons, or relaxing at one of our delightful summer concerts, there is something for everyone. We welcome families, seniors, and individuals of all abilities to create unforgettable memories with us. Check out our Aquatics Guide and join the fun—the water is perfect!

[2026 Summer Aquatics Guide (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/28722/Aquatics-Guide-Summer-2026-PDF)

* Swim program registrations begin Wednesday, March 18, 2026 at 8:30 a.m.
* Season kicks off Memorial Day weekend, May 23rd through May 25th at the Mervyn V.T. Haines Community Pool, 10 Wahler Road, adjacent to Livingston High School
* Early Bird discounts for pool memberships end Friday, May 15, 2026
* Pool Memberships available NOW! [Join today](https://register.capturepoint.com/reg/login.cfm?cuBJA%2FTClZwtomy3Erh3n%2B7aqR5PXoxk9V2eh8RZkO%2BWj9UZiY3p8g%3D%3D)
