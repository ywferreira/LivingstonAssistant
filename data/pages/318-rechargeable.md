# Rechargeable

## Bring Rechargeable Batteries to Electronics Event

Livingston accepts rechargeable batteries during its [Electronics Recycling Events](/303/Electronics-Recycling-Events).

**Accepted items include:**

* Lithium
* Li-ion
* Ni-Cad
* hearing aid "button" batteries
* other rechargeable batteries

Maximum per household is 5 lbs. No batteries over 2 lbs. or 3Ah.

**Not accepted:**

* lawnmower batteries
* leaf blower batteries
* automobile batteries
* driving toy batteries
* Alkaline and other "regular" batteries (these go in **regular garbage**)

The largest-size rechargeable battery accepted is power-tool size.

## Important  to Note

* All battery terminals must be taped over (if needed, you will be assisted at drop-off).
* Special-purpose or unusual batteries are subject to review.
