# Tennis / Pickleball Courts - Usage Guidelines

## Memberships

Memberships are required to play on Township Tennis and Pickleball Courts.

Renew or purchase your court membership at the Livingston Senior/Community Center, 204 Hillside Ave., Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.

Individuals are required to come to the Rec office in-person to take a photo and to receive their ID badge. For individuals renewing their membership, please bring your ID badge so that it may be validated each year. A valid ID is required to play on court locations.

### Annual Fees

Residents (10+): $25

Non-residents: $300

Non-resident Seniors (62+): $150

## Court Reservations

Online reservations are required to reserve Pickleball Courts 1 through 6 at Heritage Middle School.

Registered individuals can reserve for 1 hour 15 minutes per day from 9:00 a.m. to dusk. When school is in session, the hours are 3:15 p.m. to dusk.

Reservations can be booked up to two days in advance of the day you would like to play. Registered individuals can go to [CommunityPass](https://register.capturepoint.com/reg/login.cfm?cuBJA%2FTClZwtomy3Erh3n%2B7aqR5PXoxk9V2eh8RZkO%2BWj9UZiY3p8g%3D%3D) to reserve a court.

### Northland Courts

Northland Courts 1 through 4 are for Round Robin (open play) only from 9:00 a.m. to dusk.
