# Rear-Yard Pickup

Garbage and Recycling

Posted on September 03, 2024

## Reminder:

Residents with rear-yard garbage pickup **must renew each year** or the service will be discontinued.

The cost of Rear-Yard Pickup is $28.50 per month for up to three garbage cans.

Billing questions can be directed to 973-868-8496.

Pay online at [fbassoinc.com/Livingston](http://fbassoinc.com/Livingston).

For more information, visit [livingstonnj.org/RearYardPickup](https://www.livingstonnj.org/1849/16627/Rear-Yard-Pickup).
