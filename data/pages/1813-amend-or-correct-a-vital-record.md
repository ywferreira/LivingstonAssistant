# Amend or Correct a Vital Record

To amend or correct a vital record, including Birth Certificates, Death Certificates, and Marriage/Civil Unions, please contact the Bureau of Vital Statistics at 973-535-7961 Ext. 1 to schedule an appointment.

There is a $40 fee for this service.
