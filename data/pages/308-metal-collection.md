# Metal Collection

## 

Reminder: TVs and Computer screens are electronics. They will NOT be picked up as either Bulk Garbage or Metal Recycling.

## Metal Items

Metal recycling pickup includes refrigerators, stoves, dishwashers, washers, dryers, microwaves, grills, air conditioners, metal dehumidifiers, basketball hoops, treadmills, exercise bikes, metal file cabinets, patio furniture, and emptied helium tanks.

## Weekly Metal Collection

* Register up until noon on any Thursday for Metal Collection the next day, Friday, except in cases of holiday or weather-related issues
* Metal items will be picked up every Friday other than holidays or as noted on this website and Township social media, weather permitting
* Registration will be unavailable when there is a holiday
* Items are to be placed on the lawn near the curb line on Thursday, the day before the scheduled pickup
* View the [Printable Metal, Bulk, and Holiday Recycling Schedules (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/9478/Printable-Metal-Bulk-and-Holiday-Recycling-Schedules-PDF) for the Metal Collection schedule

## Register

[Request a Metal Pickup online](https://www.livingstonnj.org/FormCenter/Department-of-Public-Works-19/Metal-Collection-Request-81) and receive an on-screen confirmation and email.

Provide your name, address, and a brief description of the metal item(s) to be on the list for pick-up.

### Propane Tanks Not Accepted

Please note that we **do not pick up propane tanks**. These can be returned to wherever you typically get your propane tank refilled.

## Contact Information

The contact and pickup information on this page relates to **metal recycling only**.

**Electronics** (including TVs and rechargeable batteries) must be disposed of at an [Electronics Recycling Event](/303/Electronics-Recycling-Events).

**Paper and Commingled items** are collected by [Waste Industries](http://waste-industries.com/), which can be contacted at 908-436-1966. View [Recycling Zones (Street List)](/1071/SectionsZones-by-Street) or [additional recycling information](/289/Recycling).
