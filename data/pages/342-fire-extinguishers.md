# Fire Extinguishers

Dispose of fire extinguishers with [household hazardous waste](/343/Hazardous-Materials). Do not leave them out for garbage collection.

Essex County holds free Household Hazardous Waste Collection Days in the spring and fall.

## Collection Day

To find out when the next Collection Day will be held:

* Contact: [Essex County Utilities Authority](http://www.ecuanj.com/newsevents.html)
* Phone number: (973) 792-9060
