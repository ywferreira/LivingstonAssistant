# News Flash

# Parkinson's Support Group

Recreation & Senior Services

Posted on January 12, 2026

﻿We were thrilled to see such a wonderful turnout at our recent Parkinson’s Support Group meeting! With over 10 participants in attendance, the room was filled with shared stories, support, and community. Thank you to everyone who joined us for making it such a meaningful morning.

If you or a loved one are looking for a supportive community, we’d love to have you at our next session!

Summer Sessions have been moved to a new location.

* Thursday, July 16, 2026
* 10:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.
* Sunrise at Livingston, 290 S. Orange Avenue, Livingston, NJ

[View program flyer (PDF)](https://livingstonnj.org/DocumentCenter/View/33427/Parkinsons-Support-Group-flyer-PDF)
