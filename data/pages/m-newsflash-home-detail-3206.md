# Zumba Beach Class (ages 62+)

Recreation & Senior Services

Posted on July 13, 2026

Bring the beach vibes—no sunscreen required!

Get moving, stay active, and have fun at our Senior Adult Zumba Beach Class with one of our favorite instructors, Simone! Enjoy upbeat music, easy-to-follow dance fitness, and great company in a fun, beach-themed class!

* Wednesday, July 29
* 1:00 PM
* Livingston Senior Community Center, 204 Hillside Avenue
* Free, pre-registration is required.

Whether you're new to Zumba or already love to dance, Simone will have you moving, smiling, and enjoying every minute. It's the perfect way to stay active, connect with friends, and bring a little beach energy to your summer!

## Registration

Free, pre-registration required on [CommunityPass](https://register.capturepoint.com/reg/login.cfm?cuBJA%2FTClZwtomy3Erh3n%2B7aqR5PXoxk9V2eh8RZkO%2BWj9UZiY3p8g%3D%3D) or in person at the Livingston Senior/Community Center, 204 Hillside Avenue, Monday through Thursday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.

Questions: Call 973-535-7925, option 0, Monday through Thursday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. or [email the Recreation Department](https://mail.google.com/mail/?view=cm&fs=1&tf=1&to=recreation@livingstonnj.org)
