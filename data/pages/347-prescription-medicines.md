# Prescription Medicines

The Livingston Police Department has installed a [permanent drug drop box](/395/Permanent-Drug-Drop-Box) that will allow residents to safely dispose of unwanted drugs twenty-four hours per day, seven days a week.

* Police Department: 333 South Livingston Avenue
* LPD: [Prescription Drug Information](/389/Drug-Disposal)

## Sharps Disposal

If you have used, old, or expired sharps and syringes, you must dispose of them safely. View [instructions](/256/Syringe-Sharps-Disposal) from the Livingston Health Department.
