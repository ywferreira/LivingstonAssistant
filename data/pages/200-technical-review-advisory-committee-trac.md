# Technical Review Advisory Committee (TRAC)

## Regular Meetings

* 9:00 a.m.
* 2nd Wednesday of every month (See schedule posted)
* Livingston Town Hall  
  357 South Livingston Avenue  
  Livingston, NJ 07039

Meetings are open to the public in accordance with the Open Public Meetings Act. Public comments and/or questions shall not be accepted during the TRAC meeting. For further questions pertaining to the TRAC, please contact the Planning Department at 973-535-7954.

## Members

The Livingston TRAC provides guidance and serves to assist applicants in submitting complete applications to the Board. The TRAC consists of the following:

* Additional rotating representative from the Planning Board
* Commercial Building/Site Plan Applicant(s)
* Elected TRAC Chairman from the Planning Board
* Fire and Police Chiefs (or Designee)
* Representative from the Business Improvement District (BID)

* Representative from the Livingston Environmental Commission (LEC)
* Township Construction Official
* Township Engineer

## Commercial Building/Site Plan Applicants

All commercial/site plan applicants are encouraged to meet with the TRAC prior to being scheduled for a Planning Board/public meeting. The Planning Administrator will determine if an application requires a TRAC review.
