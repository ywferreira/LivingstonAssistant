# Dead Animals

If there is a dead animal on a Township roadway or on your property, you have two options:

* [Report a Concern](https://main.govpilot.com/web/public/e920c023-2c8_Report-a-Concern-Township-of-Livingston?uid=7126&ust=NJ&pu=1&id=0)
* Call and report it to the Police Department Non-Emergency number at 973-992-3000 ext. #3390 (Please do not call 911).

The Animal Control officer will ensure that the animal is appropriately removed.

For more information, visit the [Animal Control page](/348/Animal-Control).
