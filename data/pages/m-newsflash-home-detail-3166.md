# Healthy Eating for Older Adults

Health

Posted on June 18, 2026

The Livingston Health Department, in conjunction with Cooperman Barnabas Medical Center and Care One of Livingston, is offering a presentation on healthy eating for older adults on Wednesday, July 15, 2026 from 2:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. at Care One, 76 Passaic Avenue, in Livingston.

Join us for this presentation to learn more about maintaining healthy eating habits as we age. Discover strategies to make healthier eating choices, ensure you are getting the proper nutrient intake, and have your questions answered.

Pre-registration is required. To pre-register, email Jessica at [jekelly@livingstonnj.org](mailto:jekelly@livingstonnj.org) or call the Health Department at 973-535-7961 ext. #7110, #7109, or #7108.
