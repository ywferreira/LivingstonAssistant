# Used Motor Oil

Used motor oil should be placed in a tightly sealed container and taken to the Firestone store on Mt. Pleasant Avenue or any other service station that will accept it.

It can also be taken to [Essex County’s semi-annual household hazardous waste collections](http://www.ecuanj.com/newsevents.html) in May and October.
