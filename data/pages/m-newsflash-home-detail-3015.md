# Livingston Lancers Summer Sports Camp

Recreation & Senior Services

Posted on February 09, 2026

Individual sport camps are conducted by the Livingston High School coaching staff. All camps are specifically “geared” towards providing each participant with the ingredients for an action-packed learning and fun camp experience. Participants will have hands-on training and be supervised by the LHS Coaching Staff in each sport.

* Registration opens Wednesday, February 11, 2026 at 8:30 a.m.
* Registration Deadline: 2 weeks prior to camp start date.
* Fee: $300 per session for residents, $400 for non-residents  
  Late Fee: $20

[View Lancer Sport Camp brochure (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/24185/Summer-Lancer-Sports-Camps-2026-brochure-PDF)

## Registration

Register online at [Community Pass](https://register.capturepoint.com/reg/login.cfm?cuBJA%2FTClZwtomy3Erh3n%2B7aqR5PXoxk9V2eh8RZkO%2BWj9UZiY3p8g%3D%3D) or in person at the Recreation Office at 204 Hillside Avenue, Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.

Questions: Please call 973-535-7925, option 0, Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. or email recreation@livingstonnj.org.
