# Pool and Aquatics

## Join the Livingston Community Pool for the 2026 Season!

The Season kicks off Memorial Day Weekend, May 23th through May 25th at the Mervyn V.T. Haines Community Pool.

Northland Pool will not be open for Summer 2026.

### Registration

Beginning Wednesday, January 14 at 8:30 a.m., Renewal & New Memberships are available online at [Community Pass](https://register.communitypass.net/reg/login.cfm?cuBJA%2FTClZwtomy3Erh3n%2B7aqR5PXoxk9V2eh8RZkO%2BWj9UZiY3p8g%3D%3D) or in person at the Livingston Senior/Community Center, 204 Hillside Avenue, Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.

Weekend, weekday, half-, and full-season memberships are available.

[See Membership categories and Pool rates here (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/5796/2024-Memberships--Rates-PDF)

## Mervyn V.T. Haines Community Pool

The [Mervyn V.T. Haines Community Pool](https://www.livingstonnj.org/Facilities/Facility/Details/Mervyn-VT-Haines-Community-Pool-6) is located at 10 Wahler Road, adjacent to Livingston High School.

Amenities include accessible men’s and women’s locker rooms with private showers and dressing rooms, chair lift for the disabled, wading pool, water slide, lap pool, table games, and snack bar.
