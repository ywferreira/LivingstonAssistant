# Vacant House Information

## Home Check

Livingston residents may submit an [Unoccupied Building form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/446) when their home will be vacant for an extended period of time, such as during a vacation. The Vacant House Program enables a police officer, during his or her normal patrol and when time permits, to check vacant houses.

## Application Process

Residents need to complete a few simple steps to utilize this program:

* Complete the [Unoccupied Building Form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/446) and print it out
* Sign the [release form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/14170) and attach a $1 processing fee
* Bring the form to Police Headquarters at 333 S. Livingston Avenue

This can be done 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
