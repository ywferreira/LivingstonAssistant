# Communications

## About

The Communications staff is responsible for overseeing external and internal communications, public relations and events, brand identity, and communications and marketing strategy in order to keep our residents informed. We work to ensure that Township, Town Council, and departmental communications are helpful, inclusive, human-centered, broadly distributed, and easy to understand.

## Services

We research, design, create, manage, and oversee content relating to:

* public education
* public engagement
* Township website
* social media channels and messaging
* printed materials
* departmental projects
* systems for reporting concerns/submitting service requests
* emergency alert and important message creation and dissemination
* [Rave Alert/Smart911 Emergency Alert System](/610/Emergency-Notifications)

## Sign up for the Township's Biweekly Newsletter

The Township of Livingston's biweekly newsletter, “Livingston Wrap-Up,” highlights Livingston news and updates, in one convenient place, delivered straight to inboxes every other Friday.

Livingston community members can sign up to receive the township’s new newsletter to learn about meeting recaps, department updates and reminders, garbage and recycling information, important township updates, and more.

To sign up, visit [livingstonnj.org/NotifyMe](//livingstonnj.org/NotifyMe), then click “Notify Me Sign In” to either sign in to the website or to create an account on the website. Once signed in, click the email icon next to “Livingston Wrap-Up Newsletter” under the “Notify Me” header. Subscribers will then receive a confirmation needed email from the Township of Livingston. Click the link to confirm the subscription.

Subscribers should look out for the newsletter in their inbox every other Friday.

### Rave Alert/Smart 911 Emergency Alert System

The Township's [Rave Alert/Smart911 Emergency Alert System](/610/Emergency-Notifications) provides emergency information by phone, text, and email about town-wide high-priority events and issues. The Township strongly urges all residents and business owners to register themselves and their family members.

Having a profile not only ensures that you will receive emergency alerts but could also help emergency services to respond more effectively by providing them with crucial medical and location information.

Please note: the information in our [RaveAlert/Smart911 system](/610/Emergency-Notifications) is private and is never sold to outside entities.
