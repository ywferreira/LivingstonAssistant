## Chairperson

## Overview

The Township of Livingston Technology Committee was created in 2003. Our mission is to:

* Enhance our community website
* Improve access to municipal government and services through information technology solutions
* Provide leadership on new information technology initiatives
* Respond to the information technology needs of the community
