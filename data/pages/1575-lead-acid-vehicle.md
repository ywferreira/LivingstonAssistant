# Lead-Acid (vehicle)

Name on battery: Small Sealed Lead Acid (SSLA/Pb)

Lead-Acid (vehicle) batteries under 11 pounds are considerable rechargeable and recyclable.

Warning: Rechargeable batteries are dangerous and can cause fires. NEVER, NEVER, NEVER put any batteries in your recycling containers or in your garbage containers.

Instead, bring such batteries to an [Essex County Household Hazardous Waste Collection Day](https://www.ecuanj.com/).

For more information, visit the [Essex County Utilities Authority](https://www.ecuanj.com/essex-county-recycling/).
