# Township Manager

## About

The Township of Livingston uses the Council-Manager form of governance. The Council is an elected legislative body for the town, while the Township Manager is entrusted with overseeing local government plans and projects. We are committed to providing the community with professional customer service and communicating effectively with all our residents. The primary goal of the Township Manager and the rest of the office staff is providing a high level of customer service. The customers we serve are our Livingston residents, businesses, property owners, and employees.

## Services

* Advise the Township Council on the financial condition of the municipality
* Appoint all personnel with the exception of the Township Clerk
* Direct the day-to-day operations of all municipal functions
* Execute contracts and improvements
* Execute the laws and ordinances of the municipality
* Negotiate contracts, subject to Township Council approval
* Prepare the municipal budget for submission to the Township Council
