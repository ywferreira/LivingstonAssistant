## Responsibilities

Welcome to the Township of Livingston's Recreation & Senior Services (Rec) Department. Our mission is to provide enrichment activities to promote a healthier, happier community.

The Recreation & Senior Services Department is comprised of programming and services that encompass all community citizens, provides integrated and comprehensive services that promote the welfare of the community, the positive development of youth, support for older adults, and the strengthening of families.

### The Root of a Healthier, Happier Community

Rec brings together programming and services that embrace the diversity of our citizens. From Yoga to Mah Jong, and Swimming to Dance, we strive to offer something for everyone!  
  
Watch for the Department’s seasonal brochures and notices on this website, and announcements in the West Essex Tribune for updated and additional information. Brochures may be available at Town Hall, the Library, or the Senior/Community Center.
