# Garbage

## Garbage Collection Vendor: F. Basso, Jr. Rubbish Removal, Inc.

Livingston’s garbage collection vendor is Basso Inc.

To report a missed pickup or for assistance with any customer service issues:

* Call 973-868-8496
* Email [livingston@fbassoinc.com](mailto:livingston@fbassoinc.com)

## Garbage Pickup

Garbage collection can occur any time between 6:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m.

* **Customer Service:**Residents should contact Basso Inc. directly by calling 973-868-8496 or [emailing Basso Inc.](mailto:livingston@fbassoinc.com) with questions or to report missed pickups.
* **Containers:**Residents are responsible for supplying their own garbage containers.
* **Container Size:** Containers must not weigh more than 50 pounds, and trucks will take up to three 30-gallon containers per pickup.

### Rear-Yard Pickup

Residents who want to sign up for **or continue** back door/rear-yard pickup service must visit [F Basso Inc](https://fbassoinc.com/livingston/) to register.

## More Information

Please note: Basso Inc. is **not**responsible for picking up curbside recycling, electronics, metal items, or hazardous household waste.

For recycling-related information from Livingston, [visit our recycling page](http://livingstonnj.org/289/Recycling). Recycling guidelines, street listings and special recycling event dates can all be found there. Waste Industries is the recycling collector for Livingston.

If you have any problems or questions related to garbage pickup, please call Basso Inc. directly at 973-868-8496. Town Hall can **not**help answer garbage collection questions.
