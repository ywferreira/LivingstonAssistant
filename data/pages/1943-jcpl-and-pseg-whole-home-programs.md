# JCP&L and PSE&G Whole Home programs

Livingston's utilities providers both offer programs that can help you cut costs and make your home more energy efficient.

## JCP&L

JCP&L offers a variety of ways to save energy in your home, including appliance rebates, appliance recycling, and whole-home audits and assessments. For a full list of programs, [visit JCP&L](https://www.firstenergycorp.com/save_energy/save_energy_new_jersey/for_your_home_nj.html).

## PSE&G

PSE&G offers a variety of ways to save energy in your home, including appliance rebates, appliance recycling, and whole-home audits and assessments. For a full list of programs, [visit PSE&G](https://homeenergy.pseg.com/).
