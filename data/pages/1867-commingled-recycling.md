# Commingled Recycling

Please place items in a container at the curb no later than 6:00 a.m. on the morning of the scheduled collection. Always rinse off food waste before recycling.

## Plastics, Glass, Cans, and Cartons

* Aluminum or steel cans for food, drinks, pet food
* Clean food containers #1 through #7
* Glass bottles: clear, green, and brown glass only
* Juice boxes
* Plastic bottles for water, soda, milk, ketchup, shampoo, bleach, detergent
* Plastic takeout/salad bar containers
* Yogurt cups

### Not Accepted

* **Plastic bags of any color are no longer accepted** with commingled recycling due to changes in the recycling industry.
* **Milk & juice cartons made of waxed cardboard are not accepted** with recycling; place these in with your regular garbage.
