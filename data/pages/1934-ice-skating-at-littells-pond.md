# Ice Skating at Littell's Pond

## Current Status: CLOSED

### Updated 2/6/26

Littell's Pond is open for ice skating when weather and ice conditions permit.

Littell’s Pond is located on Wahler Road, behind the tennis courts. Parking is available in the lot for the Marvin V.T. Haines Community Pool at 10 Wahler Road, adjacent to Livingston High School.

When allowed, skating is permitted from 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

If ice conditions change, "No Skating" signs will be posted and must be followed. Skating at Littell’s Pond is at your own risk; for your own safety, always read and respect all signs posted at Littell’s Pond.

### Always use caution when skating:

* Follow all posted signs
  + Stay off of Littell's Pond when “No Skating” signs are posted
* No Town supervision is provided
* Skate at your own risk
* Use caution when skating, ice conditions may change throughout the day
* Skate with at least one other person (the buddy system)
* Avoid gathering in large groups on the ice
* Beware of ice covered with snow
* Stay away from cracks, seams, pressure ridges, and slushy areas
* Ice fishing is not permitted
