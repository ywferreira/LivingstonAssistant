# Latex

Remove the lid from the paint can and make sure the leftover latex paint is dry.

**Never dispose of wet paint in the garbage.** Doing so can result in injury to workers, damage to trucks, and paint spilled out of the garbage truck from the front of your house to the end of the block or whenever the paint can is finally empty.

Dry latex paint can be then disposed of with bulk garbage, on your last [regular garbage pickup day of the month.](/288/Garbage)

### How do I dry out latex paint?

If a small amount of paint is left and is not yet dry add kitty litter, sawdust, or strips of newspaper to the paint to speed the drying process.

If there is a large amount of leftover paint, consider donating it to a neighbor, local charity, or theatre group.

If that is not possible, pour a large amount of kitty litter into a large, heavy-duty black plastic contractor bag and slowly add the paint into the bag. As long as there is enough kitty litter in the bag the paint will be absorbed; once it's dry, put the bag and empty, dry paint can out for bulk pickup on your last regular pickup day of each month.
