# LACD Pool Party

Health

Posted on June 05, 2026

The Livingston Advisory Committee for Disabilities invites you to our Pool Party for people with special needs and their families.

Bring your swimsuit, towel, and sunscreen and come and take a plunge in the summer sun!

Pizza and snacks will be available for our special guests.

Free!

RSVP by Saturday, August 8. Go to: [tinyurl.com/LACD2026PoolParty](http://tinyurl.com/LACD2026PoolParty)

For more information, email [aotyan27@gmail.com](mailto:aotyan27@gmail.com).

## Inclement Weather

To find out if this event has been canceled due to inclement weather, email [aotyan27@gmail.com](mailto:aotyan27@gmail.com).

Rain date: Thursday, August 20 from 4:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.
