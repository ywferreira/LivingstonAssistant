# Welfare & Human Services

## About

Livingston Welfare & Human Services provides and administers benefits and entitlement programs.

## Services

* Assist residents with benefits and entitlement programs, such as:
  + NJ SNAP (previously Food Stamps)
  + Social Security/Medicaid
  + Utility payment assistance
* Assist residents with non-governmental programs
  + For example, eligible families may receive reduced rates for the Township pool and recreation programs
