# Livingston Express Shuttle

## Shuttle Service

Livingston Express Shuttle service offers round-trip transportation between the Livingston Mall and the South Orange Train station.

### Direct Contact

If you need to contact the shuttle company directly, contact:

Transdev Dispatch at 973-618-1280

### Parking

Parking for commuters is available free of charge in a designated parking area at the Livingston Mall. Riders may park their vehicles in Section 18, located near the Walnut Street entrance, behind Macy’s and beside the “bus depot,” where there are two active bus shelters.

The parking area is well lit and is regularly patrolled by both Mall security and the Livingston Police Department. The Livingston Mall region is considered to be a primary patrol district and since the addition of the Express Shuttle service, the Police Department has increased the frequency of the patrols.
