## Know How to Report an Outage

### JCP&L/First Energy

* Call 1-888-LIGHTSS (1-888-544-4877),
* Click the “Report Outage” link [online](https://www.firstenergycorp.com/), or
* Text OUT to 544487

### PSE&G

* Text OUT to 4PSEG (47734),
* Report outage [online](https://nj.myaccount.pseg.com/myservicedashboard/outage),
* Report through the [PSE&G mobile app](https://nj.pseg.com/app), or
* Call 1-800-436-PSEG (7734)
