# Recycling

## Recycling Collection Vendor: Waste Industries, LLC

Livingston's recycling collection vendor is Waste Industries, LLC.

To report a missed pickup or for assistance with any customer service issues:

* Call 908-436-1966
* [Email Waste Industries](mailto:info@waste-industries.com)

For more information, visit [Waste Industries](https://www.waste-industries.com/residential.html).

## Paper & Commingled Recycling

* Waste Industries picks up Paper and Commingled recycling on alternating weeks.
* Containers can be no larger than 32 gallons or weigh more than 50 lbs when filled.
* Waste Industries’ trucks may arrive as early as 6:00 a.m. to pick up recycling. If recycling is not out at the curb by the time trucks arrive, it will not be collected.

Residents may put recycling at the curb on the afternoon before the scheduled pickup:

* After 4:00 p.m. during Eastern Standard Time (November through March)
* After 6:00 p.m. during Eastern Daylight Savings Time (March through November)

Streets are broken into 5 recycling zones:

* Zone 1 - Monday
* Zone 2 - Tuesday
* Zone 3 - Wednesday
* Zone 4 - Thursday
* Zone 5 - Friday
