# Make it a great summer at Haines Memorial Pool

Recreation & Senior Services

Posted on February 11, 2026

There's still plenty of summer left to enjoy—and now is the perfect time to join! Livingston's Community Pool is open with hours through Labor Day, offering full, late season, and weekend memberships.

Whether you're looking to cool off, relax, or spend quality time with family and friends, Haines Memorial Pool is the place to be!

* Kiddie Splash Pool
* Lap Pool
* Water Slide
* Shaded Areas with Lounge Chairs
* Snack Shack

[See Membership categories and Pool rates (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/5796/2026-Memberships--Rates-PDF)

[View the Pool Hours flyer (PDF)](https://files.constantcontact.com/4d7b8b84901/acbe41f6-454b-43cf-9b30-e443b6f6dc57.pdf)

Soak up the rest of the summer with our Late Season Pool Membership beginning August 1 through the end of the pool season. For more information on our Pool Memberships, view the [2026 Aquatics Guide (PDF)](https://files.constantcontact.com/4d7b8b84901/e975588e-afd9-4062-8e3f-1eb81dc554e9.pdf).

Come make memories, cool off, and enjoy everything Haines Memorial Pool has to offer. We can't wait to see you at the pool!

## Registration

Renewal and New Memberships are available online at Community Pass or in person at the Livingston Senior/Community Center, 204 Hillside Avenue, Monday through Thursday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m.

Questions: Call 973-535-7925, option 0, Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. or [Email Recreation and Senior Services](https://mail.google.com/mail/?view=cm&fs=1&tf=1&to=recreation@livingstonnj.org).
