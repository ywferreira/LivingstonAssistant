# Schedule an Inspection

You can schedule an inspection by:

* filling out the [form online through the GovPilot portal](https://main.govpilot.com/web/public/81d03c4f-96e_Construction-Permit-Township-of-Livingston?uid=7126&ust=NJ&pu=1&id=0),
* calling the inspection line directly at 973-535-7953, or
* visiting the Building Department at Town Hall, 357 South Livingston Avenue, 2nd floor, between 7:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m., Monday through Friday

You must have a valid permit number in order to schedule all inspections.

### When do I need an inspection?

There are a variety of times at which you’ll need an inspection. For a complete list, see our [Required Inspection Checklist (PDF)](https://livingstonnj.org/DocumentCenter/View/45).
