# Volunteer with the Livingston Fire Department

The Livingston Fire Department and its 70+ volunteers may be generally unknown to the everyday person, but for the many who call upon us for help each day, we are never forgotten. Day or night, rain or shine, we are always on call, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year, providing emergency services for the residents of and visitors to Livingston, New Jersey.

## Qualifications to become a Livingston Volunteer Firefighter

* Be a resident of the Township of Livingston
* Be between the ages of 18 and 55
* Be able to pass a criminal background check
* Be able to pass a physical exam
* Once you become a Probationary Member, you must attend monthly meetings and drills as well as respond to fire calls.
* Be able to attend the Fire Academy, which consists of:
  + 180 hours of classroom time
  + Be able to complete a timed physical agility test at the academy
  + Complete CPR and Bloodborne Pathogens training
  + Pass written and practical exams
  + Wear personal protective equipment

The Livingston Fire Department provides equal opportunities to all of its volunteers.
