# New Biweekly Livingston Newsletter

Home

Posted on March 09, 2026

The Township of Livingston is proud to announce the creation of a new biweekly newsletter, “Livingston Wrap-Up,” highlighting Livingston news and updates, in one convenient place, delivered straight to inboxes every other Friday.

Livingston community members can sign up to receive the township’s new newsletter to learn about meeting recaps, department updates and reminders, garbage and recycling information, important township updates, and more.

To sign up, visit [livingstonnj.org/NotifyMe](http://livingstonnj.org/NotifyMe), then click “Notify Me Sign In” to either sign in to the website or to create an account on the website. Once signed in, click the email icon next to “Livingston Wrap-Up Newsletter” under the “Notify Me” header. Subscribers will then receive a confirmation needed email from the Township of Livingston. Click the link to confirm the subscription.

Subscribers should look out for the newsletter in their inbox every other Friday.
