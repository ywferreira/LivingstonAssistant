# Recycling & Reclamation Committee

The Township's Recycling and Reclamation Committee continues to research, practice, and teach ways to reduce, reuse, and recycle. They also participate in the Township's Electronics Recycling & Paper Shredding events each year.

## Recycling in Livingston

Livingston recycles the following materials:

* Batteries
* Commingled glass and plastics #1 through 7
* Light bulbs
* Metal
* Paper and cardboard
* Special/non-traditional items

## TVs, Computers, & Monitors on the Curb

New Jersey residents can **not** put TVs, computers, monitors, or other electronics out on the curb for regular garbage collection pickup. These items must be properly disposed of, either at an electronics drop-off point, such as a county or municipal solid waste collection center, or a participating electronics retail store.

To help residents dispose of electronics properly, the Township typically hosts multiple Electronics Recycling & Paper Shredding Events each year. For more information, visit the [Electronics Recycling page](/336/Electronics).
