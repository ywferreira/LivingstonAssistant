# Junior Police Academy

## What is the Junior Police Academy?

The objective of the Livingston Junior Police Academy is to create a week of education and fun through a police academy format. The curriculum includes various presentations from guest law enforcement agencies, field trips, hands-on activities, and physical training to give the recruits an idea of what it takes to become a Police Officer.

## Who can attend?

All applicants must be Livingston residents who are entering sixth, seventh, or eighth grade in the fall of 2026.

## Is there a cost?

There will be a fee of $100 per attendee.

## When is the Junior Police Academy?

The academy will be held from Monday, July 13 through Friday, July 17, 2026 from 7:45 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## How can I apply?

**This year's class is currently full and we are no longer accepting applications.**

## How can I apply to be a volunteer staff member?

We have had a tremendous response from former attendees who would like to volunteer to help us with the academy. Candidates must meet specified criteria to apply, including having previously attended the academy and be entering into tenth grade or higher in the fall of 2026. Please view the [Junior Police Academy Staff Application (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/34362) for more information.

## What if I have questions?

Please see the application for the answers to many questions. If your question is not answered by the FAQs, please contact [Officer Michael Barreiro](mailto:mbarreirol@livingstonnj.org) by email or by calling 973-992-3000 ext. #3280.
