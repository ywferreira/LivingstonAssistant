# Livingston Green Team Committee

The Livingston Green Team Committee strives to help make our Township environmentally friendly and strong.

Livingston Township is proud to be Bronze Level Certified by [Sustainable Jersey](http://www.sustainablejersey.com/about/). Sustainable New Jersey is a certification and incentive program for municipalities that want to go green, save money, and take steps to sustain their quality of life over the long term.
