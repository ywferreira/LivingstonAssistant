# Patrol Division

## About

The Livingston Police Department’s Patrol Division is responsible for the initial response to emergencies. As first responders, we also assist residents, enforce motor laws, and safeguard homes and businesses within the community.

## Initial Response

The Patrol Division is responsible for the initial response to reports of medical emergencies, accidents, fires, and crimes. Patrol officers work a twelve-hour shift that changes twice per day. As a result, the Patrol Division is organized into four squads.

### Assistance

The patrol officers are generally the first responders to any call for service by the public. Patrol officers also enforce New Jersey Criminal Statutes, New Jersey Motor Vehicle Statutes, and Township Ordinances. If a person needs the assistance of the Livingston Police Department, it is generally one of these officers who will first assist him or her.

The Patrol Division is also responsible for assisting citizens with quality of life issues and making referrals when appropriate. These officers pride themselves on rapidly responding to calls and safeguarding businesses and homes within the community.
