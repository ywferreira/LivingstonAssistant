# Privacy Policy

This policy describes the Township of Livingston’s privacy practices regarding information collected from visitors to this site. It contains information about what data is collected and how that information is used. This privacy policy applies to this website and all websites managed by the Township of Livingston, New Jersey.

We will not collect personally identifiable information about you when you visit our website unless you choose to provide that information to us. We do not give, share, sell, or transfer any personal information to a third party.

## Collection and Use of Information

### Server Log / Log Analysis Tools

The Township of Livingston utilizes server logs and log analysis tools to create summary statistics about the use of the website. The summary statistics are used for purposes such as assessing what information is of most interest to users, determining technical design specifications, and identifying system performance or problem areas. The following information is collected for this analysis.

* Name, domain and numerical internet address of the host computer (usually your ISP). This tells us which ISPs people use to connect to our sites.
* Date and time you accessed the website. This helps us make sure we have the resources to help all the people who use the site at peak times.
* Internet address of the web page you came from. This tells us how people find the site.
* Pages you visited. This tells us what is popular/helpful/interesting.
* Browser you use. This allows us to keep our site accessible to as many people as possible.

For site management functions, aggregated information is collected for analysis and statistical purposes. The Township does not report or use this information in any manner that would reveal personally identifiable information and does not release this information to any outside parties unless required to do so under applicable law.

For site security purposes and to ensure the site remains available to all users, the Township uses software to monitor network traffic to identify unauthorized attempts to upload or change information or to otherwise cause damage to the site. Anyone using this website expressly consents to such monitoring.

We track the keywords that are entered into our search engine to measure interest in specific topics, but we do not track which terms a particular user enters.

### Email

Personally identifiable information contained in a question or comment sent to the Township of Livingston in an email message or in an online form submitted to the Township will be used by the Township to respond to the request and to analyze trends. The Township may redirect the message to another Township department or person who is in a better position to answer the question. We treat email the same way we treat letters sent to the Township.

### Email Subscription List

The site provides users the opportunity to subscribe to and unsubscribe from informational mailing lists at [LivingstonNJ.org/Join](/Join). We do request information about our visitors when they utilize our mailing list form to determine which email addresses are added to or removed from our lists. Site users are free to unsubscribe at will using this form should they opt not to receive future communications from us.

The Township of Livingston has restricted discussion group lists. Neither the restricted discussion group lists nor the Township websites are a public forum for free discussion. These restricted lists exist to serve the Township’s need to make useful and practical information available and facilitates provisions of a Township service or furthers another specifically articulated purpose of the Township. Voluntary use of Township of Livingston restricted discussion email lists may reveal personal information to current and future users and administrators of these lists.

### Web-based forms

The Township uses web-based forms. Personally identifiable information collected from these forms has a specified use (e.g., registration, applications, survey response, etc.) and the information is used by the Township only for that purpose.

### Online Surveys

From time to time, online surveys are being conducted on Township’s Website. It is the Township’s policy that confidential information gathered in these online surveys is used only for the research purposes indicated in the survey. Unless otherwise noted on the specified survey, your answers are confidential and individual responses will not be shared with other parties unless required by the State of New Jersey Open Public Records Act. Aggregate data from surveys may be shared with external third parties.

### E-commerce

The Township of Livingston website enables you to pay for services online with a credit card. These transactions are encrypted. It is the Township’s policy that confidential information you enter in the transaction is used only for the purposes described in that transaction.

### Cookie Use

The Township website may use cookies to provide interactive pages, such as web-based forms or surveys. These cookies are only used to allow applications to work. The Township does not use cookies for other purposes. At no time will cookies track your activities on other websites or on your computer, or try to gather information you choose not to give from you browser or computer. The Township of Livingston does not use web bugs to track or report visitor information.

### Open Public Records Act and Legal Disclosure

All information collected from the Township of Livingston website, including the summary server log information, emails sent to the website, and information collected from web-based forms, is subject to the provisions of State of New Jersey Open Public Records Act. Information about the State of New Jersey Open Public Records Act is available from the State of New Jersey Government Records Council at [http://www.state.nj.us/opra](http://www.state.nj.us/opra/).

### Links to Non-Township of Livingston Sites

When you follow a link to a non-Township of Livingston site, even the sites of other governmental agencies, this policy will not apply. You should check the privacy policy of each of these sites if you have concerns about how they handle information.

### Changes to Privacy Policy

If the Township of Livingston makes any material changes to the way we use your personally identifiable information, we will post the changes here.

### Links from the Township of Livingston Websites

The purpose of the Township of Livingston websites is to promote the programs, services, policies, and objectives of the Township. The Township reserves the right to refuse to post any website links or to delete links already posted at any time, in conformance with this policy, without notice.  
  
Every link must undergo review to determine its relevance and appropriateness. The following criteria will be used to decide whether to grant request for Township website links.  
  
The Township will consider providing links to websites for:

* U.S., New Jersey, or Essex County governmental agencies as well as other websites maintained by or on behalf of international, national, state, regional, or local governmental or public educational agencies.
* Agencies, organizations and companies sponsoring or otherwise participating in Township activities as related to that Township activity.
* Links which provide a public service to the community with regard to emergency information, provide links to public and private utility service companies, and provide links to websites that contain pertinent life-sustaining, public safety, and public health information.
* Links to international, national, state, regional or Township professional organizations at the discretion of the departments whose activities pertain to such professions.
* Informational links provided by departments that pertain to the areas of knowledge the departments are involved in or have knowledge of. (Specifically, but not limited to, links as determined by Information Technology, the Library, Public Health, and Inspections)
* Links which promote the economic development and health of the Township. (Specifically, but not limited to, the Business Improvement District.)
* Links to agencies or websites which have awarded prizes or status to the Township of Livingston, although the Township of Livingston reserves the right to not link to award granting entities at the discretion of the departments involved.
* Links to search engines or directories or mapping engines, which provide useful information at the sole discretion of the Township of Livingston and its departments.

The Township will not provide links to websites for:

* Businesses or non-profit organizations unless they fit any of the criteria stated above.
* Promotion of illegal materials or information.
* Political campaign information or endorsements.
* Political organizations or other organizations advocating a local, state, or federal issue.
* Adult material not related to health education.
* Individual or personal homepages.
