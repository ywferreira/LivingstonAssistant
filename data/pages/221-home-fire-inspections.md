# Home Fire Inspections

## For Sale or Rental of One- & Two-Family Homes

In order to sell or rent your one- or two-family home in the Township of Livingston you must complete a Home Fire Inspection, which will be conducted by the Livingston Fire Department. This is required and you will not be able to sell your home or allow a renter to occupy the premises until the property has passed the inspection.

Please call the Livingston Fire Prevention Bureau at 973-992-2373, Monday through Friday to schedule a Smoke Detector Inspection. We kindly ask that you call to schedule an inspection 10 to 14 days prior to your closing.  
  
There is a required $50 fee for each initial inspection. For each re-inspection required following an unsuccessful initial inspection, there is an additional charge of $25. Please make all checks payable to the Township of Livingston.  
  
For further information, please view [requirements for sale or rental of one- and two-family homes](/224/Smoke-Detector-Requirements-for-Resale) or [Requirements for the Sale or Rental of a One or Two Family Home (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/351).
