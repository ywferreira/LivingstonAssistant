# Planning

## About

At the Livingston Planning Department, we work closely with residents and other infrastructure departments in order to ensure appropriate land development and redevelopment. We also review all construction and land usage in accordance with Township land ordinances.

## Services

* Provide affordable housing information
* Provide interpretation of development laws
* Evaluate master plan
* Review applications
