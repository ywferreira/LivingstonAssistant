## Passaic River Canoe/Kayak Events

We supply personal flotation devices (PFD), canoes, and paddles OR you can bring your own boat and gear. Please dress appropriately with the following: hat, boots, water shoes, or old sneakers. NO flip flops. Don't forget to bring insect repellent, sunscreen, and water.

Pre-registration required; email the [Trails & Greenways Committee](mailto:LivGreenTrails@gmail.com) to register.

All dates are weather permitting and there are no rain dates.

### 2026 Paddle Dates: Saturdays

* Due to drought conditions, the Committee can't predict when there will be enough water in the river to launch more than a week in advance. If you would like to paddle, please email the committee and we will put you on our notification list. Generally, we will know the Wednesday before if the water is high enough to paddle. High water isn't generally an issue. Heavy rain or thunderstorms will also cause cancelations.

\*Rain and low water levels will cause canceled trips.
