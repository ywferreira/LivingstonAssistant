# EV Charging

## Electric Vehicle Charging Stations

Livingston has three EV charging stations, located on the Northeast side of Town Hall at 357 S. Livingston Avenue. Anyone with an electric or hybrid vehicle can park in one of the designated spots to charge their vehicle for a fee while visiting Town Hall or a nearby location.

[EVConnect](https://www.evconnect.com/) is the NJ Department of Environmental Protection's (NJDEP) approved contractor and maintains the three dual-port level 2 charging stations. The cost of purchasing and installing the charging stations was covered by the NJDEP's *Electrify America* program. The Township is responsible for supplying and maintaining the supply of electric power to them.

### Fees

Effective on Monday, October 30, 2023, fees are as follows:

* Free: first 30 minutes
* $0.50 per kwh: 30 minutes to 4 hours
* $1.00 per kwh: 4 to 8 hours
* $3.00 per kwh: over 8 hours
* $0.50 per minute: vehicles connected but not charging (after 10-minute grace period)

Payments are made through the EVConnect App, which can be downloaded from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/search?q=ev%20connect&c=apps&hl=en_US&gl=US) or [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ev-connect/id1197712470).

Those taking advantage of the charging stations are reminded to reasonably limit their charging time so that others may also be able to use the stations.
