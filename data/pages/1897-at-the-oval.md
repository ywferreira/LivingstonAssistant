# At The Oval

## [Summer Arts Series (June)](https://livingstonnj.org/1961/Summer-Arts-Series-June)

Programs and activities at the Oval on Wednesdays in June.

## [Summer Concert Series (July)](https://livingstonnj.org/1898/Summer-Concert-Series-July)

Summer Concerts at the Gazebo, Wednesdays in July, beginning at 7:00 p.m. In the event of rain, program will be moved to Livingston High School Auditorium.

## [Summer Movie Nights (August)](https://livingstonnj.org/1899/Summer-Movie-Nights-August)

Summer Movies at the Gazebo, Wednesdays in August, beginning at Sundown. In the event of rain, program will be moved to Livingston High School Auditorium.
