# Hometown Heroes Program

## Submissions for the initial 2025 to 2027 period are closed

The Township of Livingston is proud to introduce the Hometown Heroes Program, a meaningful way to honor and recognize current and former Livingston residents who have served in the United States Armed Forces.

Through this program, we will proudly display commemorative banners throughout town as a living tribute to the brave men and women of Livingston who have selflessly served our country. Each banner will celebrate a specific individual, featuring their name, photo (preferably in uniform), branch of service, and era of service.

Note: The banners will temporarily be taken town each holiday season, starting around Thanksgiving. They will be reinstalled in January.

## Banner Details

* Size: 30" x 60"
* Cost: $250 per banner
* Display Period: The banner will be displayed for at least two years (initial period: November 2025 to November 2027) or until it shows signs of wear, after which it will be returned to the sponsor.
* Please note that there will be a limit of 45 total banners each program period. Next program period: 2028 to 2029
* Financial assistance may be available for qualifying families.

## Banner Placement

If you have a preference on placement in a specific part of town (such as the Town Center or near a memorial area), we will do our best to accommodate your request. However, banner locations cannot be guaranteed.

## Eligibility

This program is open to any current or former Livingston resident who served in any branch of the U.S. Military during a recognized era of service. Sponsors may be family members or loved ones of the honoree.

## Priority

During the next program period (2028 to 2029), priority will be given to those who were not able to get in for the initial period.
