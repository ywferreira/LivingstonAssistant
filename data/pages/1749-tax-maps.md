# Tax Maps

## Determining Which Tax Map You Need

### Step 1

The base map is broken down into five Key Maps. Using the base map, find the approximate section of Town where your property is located. Access the [base map (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/889).

### Step 2

Open the Key Map you identified in Step 1. Using the Key Map, find your street and the approximate location of your home. Find the tax map number, indicated by a symbol. Access the following key maps:

* [Key Map 1 (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/890)
* [Key Map 2 (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/885)
* [Key Map 3 (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/886)
* [Key Map 4 (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/887)
* [Key Map 5 (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/888)
