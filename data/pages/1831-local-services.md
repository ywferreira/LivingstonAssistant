# Local Services

## Post Office

For more information about the Livingston Post Office, visit [USPS](http://tools.usps.com/find-location.htm?location=1370692).

## Utilities

* Cable: Visit [Optimum](https://www.optimum.net/), [Verizon](https://www.verizon.com/), or [Xfinity](https://www.xfinity.com/overview).
* Gas: Visit [Public Service Electric & Gas (PSE&G)](http://nj.pseg.com/).
* Electric: Visit [Jersey Central Power & Light (JCP&L)](https://firstenergycorp.com/jersey_central_power_light) or [Public Service Electric & Gas (PSE&G)](https://nj.pseg.com/).
