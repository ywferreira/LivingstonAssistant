# Street Light Outage/Day-Burner Report

Street Lights help keep Livingston bright, attractive, and safe.

Our electric utilities maintain the street lights; it’s important to let them know if those street lights aren’t working properly.

## How to Report Street Light Problems

When reporting a problem, you’ll need to provide information on the location of and problem with the light.

If you see a street light that is damaged or not working properly, please report it to the proper electric utility right away:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Contact JCP&L** | **Contact PSE&G** |
| ONLINE: [Online reporting form](https://www.firstenergycorp.com/content/customer/service_requests/report-lighting-problem.html) | ONLINE: [Online reporting tool](https://nj.pseg.com/outageandgasleaks/duringanoutage/streetlightoutages) (must have an account number to report online) |
| CALL: 1-888-LIGHTSS (544-4877) | CALL: 1-800-436-PSEG (7734) |

## Have this Information Ready

Please have the following information ready in advance to make the reporting process easier:

* Your name and telephone number in case more information is needed
* Address where the streetlight is located – nearest house or business address, and nearest cross street
* The number on the pole (located at eye-level on the pole)
