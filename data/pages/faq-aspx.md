* ### Is a C.O. (Certificate of Occupancy) required for the sale of my home?

  No, a C.O. is not required. However, we recommend that you call the Building Department at 973-992-5000 ext. #5200 to determine if there are any open permits on your property, which could slow the process of selling your home. Be sure to **do this well in advance of your closing date** to ensure receiving the information in time.

  To sell your home, you **will** need a smoke detector certification, which you can obtain by calling the Livingston Fire Department at 973-992-2373.

  Building & Zoning
* ### Do I need a permit to replace my kitchen cabinets?

  No, you do not need a permit to replace only the cabinets.

  However, a permit is required if there will be any new electric or plumbing, or if any structural changes are being made.

  Building & Zoning
* ### Do I need a permit to replace my bathroom fixtures?

  As long as they are direct replacements with no change in location, you do not need a permit to replace:

  + Sink
  + Toilet
  + Tub

  Otherwise, a permit is required.

  Building & Zoning
* ### Do I need a permit to replace or install a shed?

  Maybe.

  Any shed over 200 square feet needs a building permit and zoning approval. Anything 200 square feet or smaller requires only zoning approval.

  Building & Zoning
* ### Do I need a permit to replace or install a fence?

  Yes. A Building Permit is required for any fence going around a swimming pool, spa, or hot tub. The fence must comply with the International Swimming Pool and Spa Code. You can pick up a permit package from the Building Department at 357 S. Livingston Avenue, 2nd floor, or [online](/1778/Checklists).

  A Zoning Permit is required for any fence on your property other than those listed above. You must comply with [Fence Ordinance (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/109) and file for a [Residential Zoning Permit (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/72). For more information, please call the Zoning Review Officer at 973-992-1595.

  Building & Zoning
