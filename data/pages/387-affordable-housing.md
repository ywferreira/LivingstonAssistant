[The New Jersey Housing Resource Center (NJHRC)](http://www.state.nj.us/njhrc/index.shtml) is a free, online searchable registry of affordable and accessible housing units throughout the State of New Jersey.

Recommendation when looking for affordable housing:

Put your name on many waiting lists. Lists are not shared between towns, administrative agents, etc., so putting your name on all available lists will increase your chances of finding housing.
