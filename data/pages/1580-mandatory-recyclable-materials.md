# Mandatory Recyclable Materials

## Residential

All homes, condominiums, townhouses, apartments, trailer parks, etc., including certain housing types considered institutional (i.e. senior citizen homes):

* Aluminum beverage containers
* Ferrous scrap
* Glass food and beverage containers
* Lead acid batteries (vehicle batteries)
* Leaves
* Mixed paper (i.e. junk mail, office paper, all marketable grades)
* Newspapers
* Old corrugated containers
* Plastics (#1 and #2)
* Steel/Tin containers
* Used motor oil
* White goods

## Commercial

All places of business (wholesale, retail, service, food, transportation, etc.):

* Aluminum beverage containers
* Ferrous scrap
* Glass food and beverage containers
* Lead acid batteries (vehicle batteries)
* Leaves
* Newspapers
* Office paper (all marketable grades)
* Old corrugated containers
* Plastics (#1 and #2)
* Steel/Tin Containers
* Used motor oil
* White goods

## Institutional

All government buildings, schools, colleges, hospitals, clinics, etc.:

* Aluminum beverage containers
* Ferrous scrap
* Glass food and beverage containers
* Lead acid batteries (vehicle batteries)
* Leaves
* Newspapers
* Office paper (all marketable grades)
* Old corrugated containers
* Plastics (#1 and #2)
* Steel/Tin Containers
* Used motor oil
* White goods
