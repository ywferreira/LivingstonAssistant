# Municipal Auctions

The Township occasionally auctions items that are no longer needed for public use.

All sales will be made to the highest bidder and shall be final and sold in an "as is, where is" condition, free from any guarantee or warranty. The payment terms are posted in the auctioneer's website. The Township of Livingston reserves the right to accept or reject any or all bids.

**PLEASE TAKE NOTICE** that the surplus property owned by the Township of Livingston will be sold online at public auction, on [Municibid](https://www.municibid.com/).
