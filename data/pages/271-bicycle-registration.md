# Bicycle Registration

## Crime Prevention

As part of ongoing crime prevention efforts within the Township, the Police Department wants to help residents protect their property. Therefore, the Police Department has a Bicycle Registration Program.

## Requirements

Any resident may register a bicycle with the department. When registered, a sticker will be placed on the bicycle with an identifying number. Therefore, the bicycle must be present at the registration. The registration form may be filled out in advance and brought to the registration event or appointment.

## Register a Bicycle

Contact the Community Policing Unit at 973-992-3000 ext. #3600 or [email us](mailto:jklapal@livingstonnj.org) to schedule an appointment to register your bicycle. View the [Bicycle Registration Form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/445).
