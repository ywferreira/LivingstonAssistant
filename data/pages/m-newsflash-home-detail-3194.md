# Vision 20/20 Priorities Survey is Live

Home

Posted on June 29, 2026

All residents are urged to take the Priorities Survey now at [livingstonnj.org/VisionSurvey](http://livingstonnj.org/VisionSurvey). The Livingston Vision 20/20 Committee’s eleventh community survey is now live and open for submissions. This survey covers services and matters managed by the Township.

From June 29 through July 20, residents are encouraged to fill out the eleventh survey online at [livingstonnj.org/VisionSurvey](http://livingstonnj.org/VisionSurvey). Paper copies will also be available for those without internet access at:

* Livingston Public Library, 10 Robert H. Harp Drive
* Livingston Senior/Community Center, 204 Hillside Avenue
* Livingston Town Hall, 357 S. Livingston Avenue

Your confidential feedback to this survey will provide the township with resident insight regarding what should be the township’s focus for the next 2 years. This is part of the Vision 20/20 Committee’s regular cycle of community assessments, conducted every 4 years.

To complete the eleventh survey, visit [livingstonnj.org/VisionSurvey](http://livingstonnj.org/VisionSurvey) from Monday, June 29 through Monday, July 20.

For more information or to obtain a paper copy of the survey, visit the Livingston Public Library at 10 Robert H. Harp Drive, the Livingston Senior/Community Center at 204 Hillside Avenue, or Livingston Town Hall at 357 S. Livingston Avenue.
