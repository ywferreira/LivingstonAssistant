# Oil Based

Oil-based paints and many solvents may contain lead and other contaminants. These must be disposed of properly, with household hazardous waste. Essex County holds Household Hazardous Waste Collection days twice each year, allowing residents to dispose of these potentially hazardous items safely.

Visit the [Essex County Utilities Authority website](http://ecuanj.com/) for information on dates and acceptable items. You can also call the Essex County Utilities Authority for more information at 973-792-9060.
