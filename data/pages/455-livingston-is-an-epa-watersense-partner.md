# Livingston is an EPA WaterSense Partner

The Township of Livingston is proud to be an EPA WaterSense partner.

## About the WaterSense Partnership

WaterSense aims to decrease indoor and outdoor water use through water-efficient products and water-saving practices.

The WaterSense program was established in 2006. Since then, WaterSense has helped consumers save hundreds of billion gallons of water and billions of dollars on their energy bills.

## More Information

For more information and resources to help you conserve water and save money in your home, visit the [United States Environmental Protection Agency](http://www.epa.gov/watersense). Search the complete list of [WaterSense-labeled products](https://www3.epa.gov/watersense/product_search.html).
