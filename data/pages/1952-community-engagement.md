# Community Engagement

The Township of Livingston recognizes how important community engagement is to the success of the municipality. The Township is able to make more informed decisions and tailor programs to meet the community's needs and desires based on feedback and input from our residents. The Township seeks to enhance the quality of life for everyone who lives, works, and visits Livingston.
