# Potholes & Road Maintenance Issues

Large or small, if you see a pothole that needs repair, please report it! Our goal is to keep the roadways in good repair.

## Livingston Roads

* [Report a Concern](https://main.govpilot.com/web/public/e920c023-2c8_Report-a-Concern-Township-of-Livingston?uid=7126&ust=NJ&pu=1&id=0)

## Essex County Roads

* Call 973-239-3366 ext. 2220
* Submit an [Online Service Request](http://www.ecdpw.org/online_service_request.php) to the Essex County Department of Public Works
* Roads include: Beaufort Avenue, Eisenhower Parkway, Kennedy Parkway, North & South Livingston Avenue, Laurel Avenue, East & West Northfield Road, Old Mount Pleasant Avenue, Old Short Hills Road, Passaic Avenue, Shrewsbury Drive, South Orange Avenue, and Walnut Street

## State Roads

* Report potholes to the [NJ Department of Transportation](https://www.njdotproblemreporting.com/)
* Roads include: East and West Mount Pleasant Avenue (Route 10) and Route 280
