# Animal Shelter Committee

The Animal Shelter Committee's main responsibility is to support the Animal Control Division with the animals and any issues that may arise at the shelter. Some members are specially trained and familiar with the procedures and requirements for fostering and adoptions; they may also work through the process with those who are adopting the animals. Committee members also help with any tasks that need to be done at the shelter.

This committee also addresses any questions or concerns about the shelter and works with [Friends of the Livingston Animal Shelter](/BusinessDirectoryII.aspx?BID=23) if fundraising or supplies are needed.
