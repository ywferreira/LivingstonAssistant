# Electronics

Electronic items must be recycled. They **cannot** be put out with the regular recycling or thrown out with the garbage.

## Livingston Electronics Recycling & Paper Shredding Events

Livingston typically holds multiple Electronics Recycling Events at the Livingston Senior/Community Center, 204 Hillside Avenue, each year.

For a schedule of Township Electronics Recycling Events dates, please visit the [Electronics Recycling Events page](/303/Electronics-Recycling-Events).

## Essex County Events

Essex County also holds computer and electronics recycling dates each year. Check with the [Essex County Utilities Authority](http://www.ecuanj.com/newsevents.html) for information and dates.

## Large or Heavy Electronic Items

The NJ Division of Solid and Hazardous Waste offers information about and options for properly disposing of [large or heavy electronic items](http://www.nj.gov/dep/dshw/ewaste/heavy.html) (50 plus pounds or with screens larger than 40 inches diagonally).
