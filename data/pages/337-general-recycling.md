# General Recycling

## General Recycling Collection

Regular recycling collection for [Commingled](https://www.livingstonnj.org/1867/Commingled-Recycling)(bottles and glass) and [Paper](https://www.livingstonnj.org/1868/Paper-Recycling) is done on alternating weeks throughout Livingston.

The pick up day for each resident's location is determined by a zone. You can find your zone, collection schedule and when items can be recycled on our [Schedules & Zones page](/1066/Schedules-Zones) or by calling the [vendor](http://waste-industries.com/) at 908-436-1966.
