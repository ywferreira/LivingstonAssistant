# What's Happening in Livingston

We want to know what’s happening in Livingston. So tell us through video and join the others below by participating in LTV’s new digital series, What’s Happening in Livingston! We’ll post videos here and on our Facebook page, and we’ll televise special shows on LTV as well.

If you are involved with a township committee/organization/group or a resident with something of interest to share, we invite you to submit a brief video telling us “What’s Happening in Livingston!” just like the samples you see here and on our Facebook page.  You don’t need a fancy video camera– just use your smart phone (shoot horizontally) and submit your video via [email to Livingston Television](mailto:LivingstonTV@gmail.com). We look forward to hearing What’s Happening in Livingston!
