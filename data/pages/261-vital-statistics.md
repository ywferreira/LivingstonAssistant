# Vital Statistics

## About the Bureau of Vital Statistics

The Bureau of Vital Statistics ensures that all births, marriages, civil unions, and deaths that take place in Livingston are accurately recorded locally and within the State Vital Statistics Office in Trenton. Certified copies of vital statistics records, marriage, civil union and partnership licenses, and burial permits are issued by this division.

We accept walk-in applications in order to obtain copies of vital records.

## Services

* Recordkeeping for:
  + [Births](https://livingstonnj.org/240/Birth-Certificates)
  + [Deaths](https://livingstonnj.org/1811/Death-Certificates)
  + [Marriages](https://livingstonnj.org/1814/MarriageCivil-UnionDomestic-Partnership-)
  + [Civil Unions/Partnerships](https://livingstonnj.org/1814/MarriageCivil-UnionDomestic-Partnership-)
* Track mortality statistics
* Issues permits for burials
