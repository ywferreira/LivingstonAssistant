# Personal Paper Records

The Township hosts several Electronics Recycling and Paper Shredding Events throughout the year. The events are held at the Livingston Senior/Community Center.

## Schedule

For a current schedule, please see the [Recycling page](/289/Recycling). You can also [contact the vendor directly](https://www.greenchiprecycling.com/) to arrange for drop off at a different time.

If you have a paper shredder at home, you can shred your personal records and include them with your regular paper recycling.
