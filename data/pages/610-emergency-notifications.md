## Text Messages, Emails & Phone Calls

Livingston uses Smart911 combined with the RAVE Alert system as its Emergency Alert System. This allows Township employees to quickly alert residents to emergencies and town-wide situations, including severe storm warnings, road closures or water issues in the local area.

PLEASE NOTE: Not all alerts are sent using all delivery methods. In order to receive the most up-to-date information, register to receive information through all three methods: phone call, email and text message.  
  
[**Sign up now using the Smart911 Portal**](https://www.smart911.com/smart911/ref/login.action?pa=livingstonnj)

or double-check or update your contact information:

[Log into your existing account](https://www.smart911.com/smart911/ref/login.action?pa=livingstonnj)

## Smart911

Livingston residents are encouraged to sign up for Smart911, a free and secure electronic system in which individuals can provide key information to create personalized safety profiles that are shared with 9-1-1 call takers and first responders in the event of an accident, issue, or moment of need.

[Learn more about Smart911.](https://livingstonnj.org/2021/Smart-911)
