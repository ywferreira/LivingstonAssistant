# News Flash

# Summer Nights at the Oval

Home

Posted on May 27, 2026

The Livingston Recreation & Senior Services Department presents “Summer Nights at the Oval” with Wednesday night art events in June, night concerts in July, and movie nights in August.

## Arts in the Park

Dates:

* Wednesday, June 3, 5:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.: Chalk the Walk – Calling all families and budding artists! Add your colorful drawings and imaginative creations to the sidewalks around the lower section of the Oval on Robert Harp Drive. Chalk will be provided free of charge. No registration is necessary; just bring your creativity!
* Wednesday, June 10, 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.: Asian Cultural Showcase – Come out and enjoy dance and music with friends and neighbors. Student and adult groups from our Asian and Indian communities will be represented. Admission is free, and food trucks will be available.
* Wednesday, June 17, 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.: Livingston Makers Fest — This family-friendly festival celebrates the Maker Movement, showcasing science, technology, engineering, art and math (STEAM). It is a gathering of curious individuals who enjoy learning and sharing their knowledge with others. Hands-on participation is encouraged.
* Wednesday, June 24, 7:00 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.: Livingston Symphony Orchestra — This event will be held indoors at Heritage Middle School, 20 Foxcroft Drive. Conductor Anthony Lagruth will provide a musical program suitable for all ages. Come join us for a wonderful evening.

Location: Livingston Memorial Park & Oval

Cost: FREE; no pre-registration is required.

## Concert Nights

Dates:

* Wednesday, July 8: Suit & Mai Tai - Yacht That Rocks – This high-energy, seven-piece band puts a fresh, electrifying spin on yacht rock, blending smooth ‘70s and ‘80s classics with powerful vocals, rich harmonies, and rock-driven intensity to create a crowd-igniting, full-production experience.
* Wednesday, July 15: The Cliffs — Home to New Jersey’s most versatile 8-piece cover band, The Cliffs deliver high-energy, down-to-earth fun with exceptional professionalism for any special event. **Due to unforeseen circumstances, this concert has been rescheduled to Wednesday, September 2.**
* Wednesday, July 22: Action Park — A high-energy, full-costumed tribute that recreates the wild, neon MTV era with iconic hits, retro visuals, and immersive, over-the-top performances inspired by New Jersey’s legendary Action Park.
* Wednesday, July 29: The Wag — A dynamic, four-piece pop-rock band from New Jersey known for catchy originals, rich harmonies, and engaging performances, delivering fun, family-friendly shows backed by 25 years of experience and national touring credits.

Time: 7:00 p.m.

Location: Gazebo in Memorial Park

Cost: FREE; no pre-registration is required.

## Movie Nights

Dates:

* Wednesday, August 5: Wicked for Good — Elphaba is hiding as the "Wicked Witch of the West" while she fights against the Wizard’s oppressive regime and attempts to save Oz’s Animals.
* Wednesday, August 12: GOAT - Will, underestimated due to his size, joins the Vineland Thorns, a struggling team aiming for a championship, challenging the notion that only large predators can dominate the game.
* Wednesday, August 19: A League of their Own — Sisters Dottie Hinson and Kit Keller join the Rockford Peaches, managing sibling rivalry and a cynical coach, Jimmy Dugan, to prove women's value in sports and their resilience.
* Wednesday, August 26: The Super Mario Galaxy Movie — Mario, Luigi, Peach, and Yoshi journey into space to rescue Princess Rosalina, who has been kidnapped by Bowser Jr.. Bowser Jr. plans to drain her power to build a universe-destroying weapon and free his shrunken father, Bowser.

Time: Sundown

Location: Gazebo in Memorial Park

Cost: FREE; no pre-registration is required.

Anyone with questions can call 973-535-7925, option 0 or email [recreation@livingstonnj.org](mailto:recreation@livingstonnj.org).
