# Remote Court: ZOOM

Livingston Municipal Court is conducting court virtually via ZOOM.

To schedule a court date, please contact the court at least 7 days prior to the date on your ticket for instructions.

Please view "Attending Municipal Court Remotely: What to Do and What to Expect" below.

**Interpreter:** If you need the service of an interpreter, please notify the court before your court date. Call 973-992-5000 ext. #5700 or 973-535-7970.

**Intérprete:** Si necesita un intérprete, notifíquelo al tribunal antes de la fecha del juicio. Llame al 973-992-5000 ext. #5700 o 973-535-7970.

**Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Accommodations:**If you need ADA Accommodations, please notify the court before your court date. Call 973-992-5000 ext. #5700 or 973-535-7970.
