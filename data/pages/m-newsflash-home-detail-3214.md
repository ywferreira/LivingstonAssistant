# Scheduled Water Main Work - Crest Terrace

Home

Posted on July 15, 2026

07-16-2026 - Scheduled Water Main Repairs on Crest Terrace. Possible water interruptions from 8:30AM-4:30PM.  Please plan an alternative route. If you see any air or discoloration in your water, run the cold water faucet at the sink closest to your water meter until the water is clear. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause & thank you for your understanding.
