## About

The Livingston Police Department is dedicated to serving and protecting all residents and visitors to our community. Officers across all of our divisions are available to address any issues relating to the law. We work closely with both the Township and our individual residents to provide professional service to the entire community.

## Services

* Work in partnership with members of the community
* Work in partnership with members of the Township
* Ensure working in accordance with constitutional rights to:
  + Enforce laws
  + Preserve peace
  + Reduce fear
  + Provide for a safe environment
* Maintain operational policies and procedures
* Make the best use of our manpower and financial resources
