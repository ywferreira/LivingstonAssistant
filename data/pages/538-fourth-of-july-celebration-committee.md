# Fourth of July Celebration Committee

## Overview

Each July 4th, the Livingston 4th of July Committee offers a family-friendly, day-long celebration. Events are held at Memorial Oval with additionally featured events held at the Northland Recreation Center.

The day begins with an Auto Show at 9:00 a.m. and includes contests, games, rides, cricket, music, and food throughout the day and into the evening. The afternoon features a special beer & wine café area with food trucks, and live entertainment featuring three different bands. The celebration comes to an end with a fantastic evening stage show and an explosive fireworks display that begins at about 9:15 p.m.

### Membership Details

Membership on this committee is appointed by the Livingston Township Council members at the January 1st Reorganization Meeting each year.

Those interested in being a part of the Livingston 4th of July Committee may [email Committee Chair Scott Maynard](mailto:scott.maynard2@comcast.net) or any member of the 4th of July Committee.
