# Antifreeze

Antifreeze should be placed in a tightly sealed container and delivered to a used oil recycling center or gas station.

It can also be taken to [Essex County’s semi-annual household hazardous waste collections](http://www.ecuanj.com/newsevents.html) in May and October.
