# Marriage/Civil Union/Domestic Partnership Certificates

The Livingston Health Department's Bureau of Vital Statistics maintains the records for all marriages/civil unions/domestic partnerships that take place within the Township's borders.

If you need a Marriage/Civil Union/Domestic Partnership Certificate immediately, please visit the Livingston Health Department's Bureau of Vital Statistics at 204 Hillside Avenue in Livingston.

## How Do I Get a Copy of a Marriage/Civil Union/Domestic Partnership Certificate?

1. Complete the records request form: [Vital Record Request Form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/403/Vital-Record-Request-Form-PDF)
   * Please Note: Proof of relationship may be required.
2. Provide a valid ID containing your current address.
   * [Acceptable Forms of Identification (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/435/Acceptable-Forms-of-Identification-PDF)
3. Mail or bring all documents to the Health Department's Bureau of Vital Statistics.
4. Pay the required $25 fee by mail or in person.

Certificates, records, and licenses are **only** issued through the Livingston Health Department, either in person or by mail:

Livingston Health Department  
Livingston Senior/Community Center  
204 Hillside Avenue  
Livingston, NJ 07039

## Payment

* **By Mail:** Check or Money Order only, made out to "Township of Livingston"
* **In Person:**Cash, Credit/Debit card, Check, or Money Order (we accept most major credit cards)
