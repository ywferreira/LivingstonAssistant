# Stations

The Livingston Fire Department is made up of three response stations. Commonly known to the firefighters as Headquarters Station, Northfield Station, and the Circle Station. The Fire Department currently operates four active engine companies, two reserve engines, one Truck Company, and a Rescue Company. The fire houses are placed strategically throughout town to provide a quick response for responding firefighters to each part of the township.

## Locations

* Circle Station: 389 W. Northfield Road
* Headquarters Station: 62 S. Livingston Avenue
* Northfield Station: 2 E. Hobart Gap Road

## About the Department

The Livingston Fire Department has a full-time Chief and Assistant Chief. Each station consists of a Deputy Chief. The Chief, Assistant Chief, and deputies have vehicles assigned to them by the department. The Chief, Assistant Chief, and Fire Prevention Bureau operate out of the Headquarters Station during the week.
