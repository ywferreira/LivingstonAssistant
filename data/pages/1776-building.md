## About

The members of the Livingston Building Department provide residents and business owners with information regarding local, state, and federal building codes. In addition to these services, we also provide commercial permits for businesses within the community.

## Services

* Provide guidance to residents regarding proposed structures
* Work closely with other departments to ensure appropriate use of land
* Grant permits to residents for:
  + Fences and retaining walls
  + Raised decks and patios
  + Pools, spas, and hot tubs
  + Home construction, renovation, and additions
  + Tree removal
* Grant permits to businesses that wish to:
  + Change ownership
  + Expand
  + Change signs
  + Change tenants
