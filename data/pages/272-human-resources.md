# Human Resources

## About

The Livingston Human Resource Department employees work to create an inclusive and respectful workplace. We respond to any issue in a timely and professional manner. We are committed to providing a work environment that promotes honesty, trust, and open communication.

## Services

* Help with issues relating to staffing and Township employment
* Enforce personnel policies and procedures
* Assist with information about benefit programs
* Perform training and development
* Handle compliance issues
