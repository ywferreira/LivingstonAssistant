# Municipal Court

## Mission Statement

We are an independent branch of government constitutionally entrusted with the fair and just resolution of disputes in order to preserve the rights and liberties guaranteed by the Constitution and the laws of the United States and this state.

## Vision Statement

* Provide equal access to a fair and effective system of justice
* Offer complementary methods of dispute resolution
* Provide quality service that continuously improves
* Maintain the independence of the Judiciary
* Acknowledge and enhance the potential of every person in our organization
* Share a common sense of identity and purpose
* Earn the respect and confidence of an informed public

**Notice to Attorneys:** Please upload all letters of representation via [JEDS/eCourts.](https://portal-cloud.njcourts.gov/prweb/PRAuth/CloudSAMLAuth?AppName=ESSO) When submitting your letter, the filing should be **Notice of appearance**.

**Interpreter:** If you need the service of an interpreter, please notify the court before your court date. Call 973-992-5000 ext. #5700 or 973-535-7970.

**Intérprete:** Si necesita un intérprete, notifíquelo al tribunal antes de la fecha del juicio. Llame al 973-992-5000 ext. #5700 o 973-535-7970.

**Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Accommodations:** If you need ADA Accommodations, please notify the court before your court date. Call 973-992-5000 ext. #5700 or 973-535-7970.
