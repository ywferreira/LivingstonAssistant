# Youth Appreciation Week

## YAW 2025

Join us Sunday, September 14 through Sunday, September 21 for this week-long series of events and celebrations for our youth and the entire family, with the week's celebrations culminating with Family Festival Day, a Town-wide Campout, and Movie Under the Stars!

### Our Youth

Year after year, our young residents get involved in all aspects of Livingston life: from working with seniors and people with special needs, to helping make the town more "green," to long-term planning. They are committed and can be counted on to volunteer and serve the community. That’s why for 29 years Livingston Township has hosted special events to honor them!

The Recreation & Senior Services Department and Healthy Community Healthy Youth (HCHY) are proud to host an exciting variety of youth appreciation events.
