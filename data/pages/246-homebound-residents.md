# Homebound Residents

Are you or do you know someone who is homebound and would need help evacuating during an emergency?

If so, complete the [Homebound Residents Form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/412/Homebound-Resident-Form-PDF).

The Health Department and the Office of Emergency Management maintain a list of homebound residents who may need assistance evacuating their homes in the event of an emergency. If you are or know of a homebound Livingston resident, please complete the form and send it to:

## Emergency Preparedness

Visit [Ready](https://ready.gov/) for important guidelines and information that can help ensure that you are -- or any homebound resident is -- better prepared in case of a disaster or emergency.

Register for [Livingston's Emergency & Police Alerts](/610/Emergency-Police-Alerts) to be sure you receive official updates, instructions, and safety information directly from the Township in case of an emergency.
