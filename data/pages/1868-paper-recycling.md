# Paper Recycling

## Paper Recycling

Please place items in a container at the curb no later than 6:00 a.m. on the morning of the scheduled collection. Visit [Schedules and Zones](/1066/Schedules-Zones) to find your recycling zone.

Paper recycling does **not**include:

* Boxes with foam packaging still inside
* Milk or juice cartons made of waxed cardboard
* Stickers
* Tissues or napkins, waxed paper
* Used paper towels

## Newspaper, Box, Paper, and Cardboard Recycling includes:

* Books - paperbacks; remove cover from hard cover book (discard hard cover in garbage)
* Brown paper
* Bulk mail
* Cardboard\*
* Cardboard egg cartons
* Catalogs
* Cereal boxes
* Gift boxes
* Magazines
* Newspapers
* Office paper
* Paper bags - non-shiny
* Pasta boxes
* Phone books
* Pizza boxes - no waxed paper; clean cardboard only -- grease-stained boxes are garbage
* Shirt cardboard

\* Always flatten cardboard boxes.
