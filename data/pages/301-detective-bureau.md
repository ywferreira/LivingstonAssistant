# Detective Bureau

## About

The Livingston Police Department’s Detective Bureau is responsible for the investigations of specific types of crimes within the municipality. Our detectives work closely with the community, other local police departments, state agencies, and federal agencies.

## Personnel

The Detective Bureau consists of a detective sergeant and six detectives. The bureau is run by a detective lieutenant, and it follows up on all crimes reported to our department. Within our bureau, individual detectives have been trained to investigate specific types of crimes.

## Areas

Presently, we have detectives assigned to the areas of:

* Arson
* Domestic violence
* Evidence and property management
* Fraud
* High priority arrest warrants
* Identity theft
* Megan’s law processing
* Narcotics
* Obtaining sworn statements as basis for criminal complaints
* Processing and photographing of all crime scenes
* Robberies
* Sex crimes
* Weapons confiscations

Two additional detectives are under the umbrella of the Detective Bureau. These detectives operate under the supervision of a detective sergeant, and they follow up on crimes committed by juveniles and family offense issues.

## Community

Our detectives work closely with other local police departments, state agencies, and federal agencies. Members of the bureau also coordinate the departments’ cases with the Essex County Prosecutor’s Office, the New Jersey Division of Child Protection and Permanency, and the New Jersey Office of the Attorney General.
