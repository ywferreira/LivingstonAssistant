# Rear-Yard Pickup

Basso Inc. offers an optional back door/rear-yard garbage removal service for a fee of $28.50/month.

Residents who want to sign up for or continue rear-yard pickup service must call 973-868-8496 or visit [fbassoinc.com/Livingston](http://fbassoinc.com/Livingston) to register.

## Payment

Your payment will be processed through monthly auto-pay using your credit card. If you wish to arrange for a different payment schedule (e.g. annual, quarterly, etc.) contact Basso Inc. directly.

## Renewal

Remember that you must renew your rear-yard pickup service as specified in your contract with Basso Inc.
