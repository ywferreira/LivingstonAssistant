# Open Public Records Act (OPRA)

## Making a Request

Use our[Open Public Records Act (OPRA) Request Form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/26261/NEW-OPRA-FORM-9_3_2024-Fillable-with-Cover-Sheet) to request public records from the Township of Livingston. Please forward all completed OPRA requests via [email](mailto:OPRA@livingstonnj.org).

A response will be made to the requestor as soon as possible, but not later than seven business days after the request is received. If you are seeking records for "a commercial purpose" as defined in N.J.S.A. 47:1A-1.1, you shall receive a response as soon as possible, but not later than 14 business days.

Pursuant to N.J.S.A. 47:1A-1.1., there is no cost to have documents emailed or faxed to the requestor. Fees for copies are based upon the size of the document to be copied and may include $0.05 per page for letter-size pages, $0.07 per page for legal-size pages, and the actual costs for other materials (CD, DVD, etc.). Delivery/postage fees may apply, depending upon the delivery type. A special service charge may apply, depending on the nature of the request.

To learn more about the Open Public Records Act, visit the [Government Records Council](https://www.nj.gov/grc/).
