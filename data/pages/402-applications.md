# Applications

All Planning Board applicants are encouraged to meet with the [Technical Review Advisory Committee (TRAC)](/200/Technical-Review-Advisory-Committee-TRAC) prior to being scheduled for a public hearing. The Planning Administrator will determine if an application requires a TRAC review.

## Accessing Forms, Checklists, & Applications

The Planning Department’s new applications, forms, and checklists are fillable PDF forms. Download/save the form you need and open it outside your web browser in Adobe Reader or other Adobe programs prior to completing the information. Edits to forms cannot be saved if completed in the web browser.

Please make the appropriate copies of the materials as required in the Submission Requirements. Please contact the Planning Department at 973-535-7954 with any questions. Please note that a [W9 form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/704) must accompany all escrow deposits.
