# Police Records Division

## About

The Police Records Division provides residents with records regarding motor vehicle accidents and public crime records, and provides handicap parking placards to those in need of one. The division is also responsible for firearm applications and registration.

## Motor Vehicle Accidents & General Complaint Reports

Accident Reports and General Complaint Reports are available for a fee.

Generally, records will be available during normal business hours within five business days. If a requested document is not a public record, you will be advised. View [Police Records Fee Schedule (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/385).

## Public Records

The term “public record” generally includes those records determined to be public in accordance with NJ.SA. 47:1A-1. For additional information, visit the [State of New Jersey](http://www.nj.gov/nj/index.shtml).

## Temporary Handicap Parking Placard

You may obtain a temporary handicap parking placard through the Police Records Division. You must obtain an application from the division and have your physician certify the application.

The application must be certified by a licensed physician, or it will not be accepted. Return the completed application to the Police Records Division along with a $4 check or money order made out to: New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission. For more information, please contact 973-992-3000 ext. #3400.

Please note that permanent handicap parking placards may only be obtained through the [New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission](http://www.state.nj.us/mvc/).
