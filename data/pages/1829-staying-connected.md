# Staying Connected

## Calendar Information

For dates of Township Council meetings, garbage and recycling pickup dates, and Township events view [the Township Meetings & Events Calendar](https://livingstonnj.org/calendar.aspx).

## Emergency & Police Alerts

Livingston uses the [Rave Alert/Smart911](https://www.smart911.com/smart911/registration/registrationLanding.action) system to send out important messages and critical information.

[Register for phone, email, and text alerts.](https://www.livingstonnj.org/610/Emergency-Notifications)

## Local News Outlets

* The West Essex Tribune -- Livingston's local newspaper providing weekly coverage of our entire community -- including schools, business, activities, meetings, special events, volunteers, and more:
  + available in print (973-992-1771) and
  + online at [West Essex Tribune](http://westessextribune.net/).
* [TAP Into Livingston](https://www.tapinto.net/towns/livingston) -- online information about activities and goings on in town.
* [Livingston Patch](http://patch.com/new-jersey/livingston) -- online information about activities and goings on in town.
