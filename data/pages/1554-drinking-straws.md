# Drinking Straws

## Drinking Straws are NOT Recyclable

Plastic straws are so small and lightweight that they easily drop through sorting screens at recycling plants. They then end up contaminating recycling loads or getting disposed of as garbage anyway.

Drinking straws should be thrown out with **regular garbage**.
