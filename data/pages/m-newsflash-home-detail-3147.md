# 2026 Summer Adult and Senior News & Programs

Recreation & Senior Services

Posted on May 28, 2026

Livingston Recreation & Senior Services is thrilled to unveil our brand-new lineup of Summer Adult & Senior programming! Whether you’re looking to spark a new hobby, elevate your wellness, or catch up with friendly faces, we truly have something for everyone.

﻿From high-energy fitness classes and trivia nights to our first-ever Adult Summer Camp and a Zumba Beach Party, we're bringing you endless ways to stay active, inspired, and connected all season long.

Registration opens Wednesday, June 10, at 8:30 a.m. for residents and non-residents.
