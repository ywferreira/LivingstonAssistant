# Summer and Seasonal Jobs with Recreation

## Summer Camp Employment

### Camp Counselor Job Open House

Thursday, March 26, 2026, 5:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at Livingston Senior/Community Center, 204 Hillside Avenue.

### Camp Dates:

Wednesday, June 24 through Friday, August 7, 2026  
(No camp Friday, July 3)

All applicants must complete a [Seasonal Employment Application (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/9426/Summer-Seasonal-Employment-Application-PDF) and provide a photo/scan of your photo ID and/or student ID as attachments.

Camp employees must be entering 10th grade, Fall of the current calendar year. Counselors in Training must be entering 9th grade, Fall of the current calendar year.

**Application Deadline:** March 26, 2026

### Availability

All camp staff will be required to work the full SIX weeks! Vacation days will not be granted. Excused absences may be granted for things such as college orientation and doctor’s appointment. Proper paperwork is required for these requests with approval from the Camp Director.

Questions: [email the Recreation Department](mailto:recreation@livingstonnj.org)

## Snack Bar Concessionaires

All applicants must complete a [Seasonal Employment Application (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/9426/Summer-Seasonal-Employment-Application-PDF) and provide a photo/scan of your photo ID and/or student ID as attachments.

Must be at least 15 years old by May 1, 2026.

### Availability

Availability during most weekends throughout the summer is required.

Questions: [email the Recreation Department](mailto:recreation@livingstonnj.org)

## Lifeguards

All applicants must [email the Recreation Department](mailto:recreation@livingstonnj.org) a completed [Seasonal Employment Application (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/9426/Summer-Seasonal-Employment-Application-PDF) and a photo/scan of your student ID as attachments. Please indicate which position you are applying for in the subject line of the email.

**Application Deadline**: March 26, 2026

Lifeguard candidates should also send a copy of their Lifeguard Training and CPR/AED Certifications.

Must be at least 15 years old by May 1, 2026.

### Lifeguard Certification

Individuals seeking employment as lifeguards at Livingston Community Pools must be certified prior to applying for a summer job.

For information about New Jersey locations for American Red Cross classes, new and re-certification students, visit [NJ Lifeguard](http://njlifeguard.com).

### Availability

Lifeguards must be available to work **every** weekend during the pool season and must be available to work through Labor Day Weekend.

Adherence to the attendance policy in the lifeguard handbook is required, including giving required notice for absences.

## More Information

### Age Requirements

All minors under the age of 18 who work in New Jersey must have an employment certificate – also called “working papers.” We will provide the form to employees under 18 who are offered a position and accept, along with other new hire documents. Please note that returning employees under 18 will need to complete working papers each year (even if they were completed previously). [More information: Employment Certificates (State of New Jersey)](https://www.nj.gov/labor/youngworkers/find-a-job/working-papers/getstarted.shtml)
