# Livingston 9/11 Memorial

## 9/11 Ceremony of Remembrance

The Township of Livingston's annual 9/11 Ceremony of Remembrance will be held on Friday, September 11, 2026, at the 9/11 Living Memorial Garden located on the Livingston Oval. The program will begin at 4:00 p.m. and will be held rain or shine. All are invited and encouraged to attend as we honor those lives stolen from us on that day 25 years ago.

## Remembrance

We remember the Livingston residents whose lives were taken on September 11, 2001:

* Luke A. Dudek
* Jeffrey Brian Gardner
* Donald Thomas Jones II
* Ming-Hao Liu
* Joseph P. McDonald
* John M. Pocher
* Kenneth Albert Zelman
