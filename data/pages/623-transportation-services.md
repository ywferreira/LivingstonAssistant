# Transportation Services

## Commuter Transportation

[Livingston Express Shuttle](https://livingstonnj.org/1915/18250/Express-Shuttle) service offers round-trip transportation between the Livingston Mall and the South Orange Train station.

## Senior Transportation

Livingston's [Senior Transportation Program](https://livingstonnj.org/815/Senior-Transportation-Services) provides transportation to any location in Livingston for residents 62 years of age and older and  for disabled residents 18 years of age and older.
