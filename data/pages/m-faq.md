* ### Who do I contact if I have questions or problems with my garbage pickup?

  Livingston's garbage collection vendor is [Basso Inc.](http://fbassoinc.com/livingston)

  Contact Basso Inc. with any issues or questions regarding garbage pickup, including missed pickups:

  + Call 973-868-8496
  + Email [livingston@fbassoinc.com](mailto:livingston@fbassoinc.com)

  To sign up for Rear-Yard / Back Door Pickup Service, please visit [fbassoinc.com/livingston](http://fbassoinc.com/livingston) or call 973-868-8496.

  Those at Livingston Town Hall cannot typically help answer questions about garbage.

  [More information about garbage collection](/288/Garbage)
