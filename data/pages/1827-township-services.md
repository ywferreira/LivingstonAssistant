# Township Services

## Birth Certificates

[Birth Certificates](/240/Birth-Certificates) are available from the Bureau of Vital Statistics located in the Health Department.

## Death Certificates

[Death Certificates](/1811/Death-Certificates) are available from the Bureau of Vital Statistics located in the Health Department.

## Finance & Taxes

[View and pay property taxes and utility bills online.](https://wipp.edmundsassoc.com/Wipp/?wippid=0710)

More information about the [Finance Department](/164/Finance).

## Garbage Collection

Information about [bi-weekly garbage collection](/288/Garbage).

## Leaf Collection Program

Information about Livingston's [Leaf Collection Program](/385/Leaf-Collection).

## Marriage/Civil Union Licenses

Marriage/Civil Union Licenses are available from the [Bureau of Vital Statistics](/261/Vital-Statistics) located in the Health Department.

## Overnight Parking

[Overnight parking](/faq.aspx?qid=80) is not allowed in Livingston Township.

## Pet Licenses

[Dog and cat licenses](/238/Animal-Licenses) must be renewed annually by January 31.
