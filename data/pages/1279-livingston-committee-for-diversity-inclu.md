# Livingston Committee for Diversity & Inclusion (LCDI)

## Our Mission

The Livingston Committee for Diversity & Inclusion (LCDI) celebrates the cultural heritages and backgrounds of our residents. We seek to raise awareness, foster respect, learn from each other, be inclusive, and together create a warm, welcoming hometown experience for all of our community members.
