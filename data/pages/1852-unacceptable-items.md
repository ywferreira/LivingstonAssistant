# Unacceptable Items

## These items are NOT acceptable in curbside garbage or bulk pickup:

* Ashes
* Automobile parts
* Concrete
* Designated recyclable material per Township Ordinance 40-2008
* Electronics
* Hazardous/combustible/caustic materials
* Logs
* Materials resulting from demolition, alterations, or repairs to buildings or other structures
* Other appliances/scrap metal
* Rocks
* Sod
* Soil
* Stones
* Stumps
* Trees (other than branches in bundles)
* Wooden Pallets

## Where to dispose of these materials

* For electronic disposal, visit the [Electronic Recycling](https://www.livingstonnj.org/303/Electronics-Recycling-Events) page
* For metal disposal, visit [Metal Collection](https://www.livingstonnj.org/308/Metal-Collection) page
* For hazardous/combustible material disposal, visit the [Essex County Utilities Authority](http://ecuanj.com/) website for upcoming events
  + For organic or construction materials, it is recommended you contact the organization that left them behind
