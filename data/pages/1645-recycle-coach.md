# Recycle Coach

## About

Livingston has partnered with Recycle Coach to provide residents with disposal information and scheduling for the Township's recycling program. Residents can discover local garbage and recycling disposal information for thousands of household items for free. Through Recycle Coach, we continue our commitment to educating residents and building better recycling habits.

Download the free Recycle Coach app in the [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/recycle-coach/id1071714967) or [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=mobi.recyclecoach.worldster.pack&pli=1).

With Recycle Coach, residents can:

* Find the closest recycling and disposal facility for any household item based on their zip code
* Become recycling experts with the “What goes where?” search tool, complete with local recycling and disposal info, as well as collection requirements for common and unique household items
* Test their knowledge with weekly ‘We Wanted to Know’ quizzes that leverage local data to increase recycling confidence
* Stay organized with a customized curbside collection and events calendar
* Receive collection reminders and real-time updates on service disruptions
* Use the problem reporting tool, equipped with geolocation, to notify municipal customer service representatives about issues like a missed collection
