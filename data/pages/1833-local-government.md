# Local Government

## Livestream Meetings: Township Council, and Zoning and Planning Boards

Council meetings, Zoning Board, and Planning Board meetings are livestreamed via our [Facebook page](http://facebook.com/LivingstonTownshipNJ).

Find more information about viewing and participating in meetings on our [Facebook page](http://facebook.com/LivingstonTownshipNJ) and on the [website calendar](https://livingstonnj.org/calendar.aspx?CID=26,35&showPastEvents=false) a few days prior to each meeting.

## Township Council Meetings & Officials

Township Council meetings are typically held every other Monday night. To find meeting dates, visit the [website calendar](https://livingstonnj.org/calendar.aspx?CID=35&showPastEvents=false).

Find information about your [Township Council members](/720/Township-Officials).
