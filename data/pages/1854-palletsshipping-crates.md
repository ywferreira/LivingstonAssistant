# Pallets/Shipping Crates

Wooden Pallets/Shipping Crates are **not** collected by the Township’s solid waste collector. Wooden pallets/shipping crates must be brought to the DART Transfer Station at 540 Doremus Ave, Newark, NJ 07105.
