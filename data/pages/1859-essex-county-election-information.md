## Essex Votes

The Essex County Clerk has introduced Essex Votes, a new election app, as a guide to voting in Essex County.

Essex Votes has all the information you need going into the upcoming election season, including:

* Voter Registration: Easily access voter registration information.
* Poll Worker Info: Find details on becoming a poll worker.
* Early Voting & Polling Locations: Locate Early Voting and Polling locations.
* Vote by Mail Info: Get guidance on voting by mail including deadlines and procedures.
* Election Results: Stay updated with real-time election results.

Essex Votes is available for download on the [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.essexclerk.essexvotes) or [Apple App](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/essex-votes/id6479920446) store.
