# Memorial Day Parade

## Remembrance Ceremony

Join your friends & neighbors at Memorial Oval across from Town Hall for a Memorial Day Remembrance Ceremony at 9:30 a.m. followed by the annual Memorial Day Parade at 10:00 a.m. on Monday, May 25, 2026.

In the event of rain, the ceremony will be held in LHS auditorium.

Look for updates on our social media:

* [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/LivingstonTownshipNJ)
* [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/livingstontownshipnj/)
* [X (Twitter)](https://x.com/Livingston_NJ)

Community organizations interested in marching in the parade must complete the [Registration Form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/13594/2024-Memorial-Day-Parade-Registration-Form-PDF) and return it by May 13, 2026.
