# Fluorescent Light Bulbs

Never throw Compact Fluorescent Light Bulbs (CFLs) or any fluorescent bulb in the garbage. They contain a small amount of mercury, which will contaminate the landfill and seep into the soil. From there, it can contaminate food or water supplies.

Livingston's Annual [Electronics Recycling and Paper Shredding Events](/303/Electronics-Recycling-Events) accept the following fluorescent bulbs:

* 4- and 2-foot bulbs
* U-shape
* Round
* CFL

CFLs can also be taken to the Essex County Household Hazardous Waste collection events in the spring and fall.
