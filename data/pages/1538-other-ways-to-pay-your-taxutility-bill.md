# Other Ways to Pay Your Tax/Utility Bill

Would you prefer to pay online? [Pay your Tax/Utility bill online](/1409/TaxUtility-Lookup-Payment).

## In Person

If you prefer to pay in person and want a receipt, bring your water/sewer bill with you when you come to the Tax/Water Collection counter on the first floor of Livingston Town Hall at 357 S. Livingston Avenue. Otherwise, your canceled check will be your receipt.

## By Check

Make payable to the “Township of Livingston” and include the block, lot, and account number on your check to ensure timely posting of your payment.

## By Mail

Make your check payable to “Township of Livingston,” and include the block, lot, and account number on your check along with the proper billing stub. If you would like a receipt, send in the entire bill along with a self-addressed stamped envelope. If no receipt is desired, mail only the payment stub with your check. The canceled check will be your receipt.

## Drop-Off Box

An after-hours payment drop-off box is located near the curb at the back southeast corner of Livingston Town Hall at 357 S. Livingston Avenue. Any payments dropped in the box after 8:30 a.m. will be posted to the account the next business day.

### No Cash Please!

Please read the back of your water/sewer bill; it contains information that could be valuable to you, and it may also answer some of your questions.

## More Information

### Are You Moving?

If you are moving, a final water meter reading is required. The reading must be scheduled before your closing date.

Please schedule a reading by filling out the form below:

* [Final Water Reading Request Form](/FormCenter/Water-Department-18/Final-Water-Reading-Request-Form-72)
  + Please note that the Township **will****only accept final reading requests via this form**.

Once we have received your completed form, we will confirm receipt and ask for additional information if required. Please submit the form **at least 48 hours** in advance of the final reading request date to ensure the final reading can be completed on your requested date.**Please note that****s****ame-day final readings cannot be performed.**
