# Livingston Advisory Committee for Disabilities (LACD)

## About Us

The Livingston Advisory Committee for Disabilities (LACD) provides programs for people with special needs. All programs are free of charge.

## Mission

To improve the lives of people with disabilities and their families. [View the LACD Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/lacdnj) for more information.

## Ongoing Events

* After-School Sports Clinics
* Bowling Program
* Arts & Crafts Program
