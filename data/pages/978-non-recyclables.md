# Non-Recyclables

These items are regular garbage and should **never** be put in with recycling:

* Bagged pet waste
* Bags with shiny coating
* Boxes with foam packing still inside (remove foam and discard in garbage; recycle box)
* Diapers
* Dishes and glasses
* Food waste (can be composted)
* Stickers
* Used paper towels, tissues, or napkins
* Waxed paper
