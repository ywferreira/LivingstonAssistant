# Essex County Hazard Mitigation

## Essex County updates its Hazard Mitigation Plan (HMP) every 5 years

This plan enables participating jurisdictions to be eligible for federal funding to rebuild stronger after disasters have struck. Read the full Hazard Mitigation Plan at [Essex County Sheriff's Office](https://www.essexsheriff.com/oem/all-hazard-mitigation-plan/).
