## Hard Copies

Hard copies of the report are available upon request. To request a hard copy, residents may [email the Water Department](mailto:water@livingstonnj.org) or call 973-535-7951. They are also available for pickup at Town Hall, 357 South Livingston Avenue, the Livingston Senior/Community Center, 204 Hillside Avenue, and at the Livingston Public Library, 10 Robert H. Harp Drive.
