# Licenses & Permits

## Door-to-Door Solicitation

[Door-to-door solicitors/peddlers](https://ecode360.com/10300268#10300268) in Livingston Township must obtain a license. Please submit the completed [Solicitation Permit Application (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/12202) to the Municipal Clerk’s office. Licenses are annual and expire December 31st each year. A letter from your employer is required, and must state that a background check has been successfully passed, and that the employer assumes responsibility for the actions of their employee.

Once the license is issued by the Township, the second page of the license will be required for you to maintain in your possession while going door to door and should be available upon request by a resident. There is a strict ["Do Not Knock" Policy (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/7570) that must be adhered to or fines and/or suspension of the license may result.

### Door-to-Door Solicitation Hours:

**Commercial** solicitors/peddlers may only visit homes beginning at 10:00 a.m. and ending one hour after sunset from October 1st through March 31st and at sunset throughout the rest of the year.

**Non-commercial,** religious and/or political canvassers and solicitors protected by the First Amendment to the U.S. Constitution shall be permitted to perform those activities without a license, 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. seven days a week.

## Filming in Livingston

Entities seeking to film in Livingston must complete a Township filming application and submit it to the Town Clerk's office. Visit the [Filming in Livingston page](/1573/LTown-Film) for more information.

## Garage and Estate Sales

No garage or estate sale fee is required. However, you must register your garage or estate sale.

Complete the [Garage/Estate Sale Registration Form (PDF)](https://livingstonnj.org/DocumentCenter/View/162/Garage-Sale-Registration-Form-03-06-2023-Fillable-PDF) and return it by emailing the [Clerk's Office](mailto:townclerk@livingstonnj.org) or delivering it to Town Hall.

View the [Garage Sale Ordinance (PDF)](https://livingstonnj.org/DocumentCenter/View/161/Garage-Sale-Ordinance-PDF) for more information.

## Landlord Identity Registration Application

Pursuant to N.J.S.A. 46:8-28 and 46:8-29, the [Landlord Identity Registration Application](https://main.govpilot.com/web/public/591394c4-d39_Landlord-Identity-Registration-Township-of-Livingston?uid=7126&ust=NJ&pu=1&id=0) is required to be given by landlords to tenants in single unit dwellings and in two – unit dwellings that are not owner-occupied and to be filed in the office of the clerk of the municipality in which any such single unit dwelling or two-unit dwelling is situated.

[Fill out the Landlord Identity Registration Application.](https://main.govpilot.com/web/public/591394c4-d39_Landlord-Identity-Registration-Township-of-Livingston?uid=7126&ust=NJ&pu=1&id=0)

## Liquor Licenses

All liquor licenses are currently accounted for. Licenses must be renewed yearly by the license holder. The term is from July 1st to June 30th. The State also offers other types of liquor licenses to qualified non-profit organizations. For more information about liquor licenses, visit the [NJ Division of Alcoholic Beverage Control](https://www.njoag.gov/about/divisions-and-offices/division-of-alcoholic-beverage-control-home/).

## Raffle and Bingo Licenses

Any nonprofit organization may apply for a raffle or bingo license for any event that is being held in the Township of Livingston. The applicant must have a current bi-annual registration ID from the State in order to apply for a license.

See the [Raffle Application Packet (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/7561) or the [Bingo Application Packet (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/15126); complete and submit the application in triplicate to the Municipal Clerk's office. Please note that the requirements for the Background Checks have recently changed. Be sure to include the [Background Check Form (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/317) with the application and follow the steps in the [Criminal History Record Electronic Filing Instructions (PDF)](https://www.livingstonnj.org/DocumentCenter/View/34779/Background-Check-Instructions). The Background Check Form must be completed by the applicant's local (hometown) police department.

## Taxi/Limousine Service

All drivers must submit a [Limousine/Taxi Driver Application (PDF)](/DocumentCenter/View/8230). Please also be sure to include the [Taxi/Limo Driver Background Check Form (PDF)](https://livingstonnj.org/DocumentCenter/View/34782/Background-Check-Application-for-Limo-Taxi) with the application and follow the steps in the [Criminal History Record Electronic Filing Instructions (PDF)](https://www.livingstonnj.org/DocumentCenter/View/34779/Background-Check-Instructions). With the completed background check from Livingston Police Department, please also include and submit a $5 processing fee to the Municipal Clerk’s office. Applicants must also file their Certificate of Liability Insurance with the Municipal Clerk’s office. View the [Taxi and Limousine Code](https://ecode360.com/10301088).
