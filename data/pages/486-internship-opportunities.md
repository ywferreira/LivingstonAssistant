## Recreation & Senior Services Internship

The Recreation & Senior Services Department provides recreational and leisure opportunities to 30,000 residents on a year-round basis. Interns have the opportunity to work closely with our staff in developing programming and coordinating events while learning the role our Department plays in the community.

### Challenging & Rewarding

If you want a service-oriented career that’s challenging and rewarding, we’re waiting to hear from you! Internships are non-paid and must be for college credit. For more information, call 973-535-7925, option 0.
