# Apps

Find links to apps associated with the Township. Click the links to open the website or app store to download the app.

### eCode 360

Search the Township's Code online.

* [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/search?q=ecode%20search&c=apps&hl=en_US&gl=US)

### Essex Votes

Your source of information for voting in Essex County.

* [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.essexclerk.essexvotes)
* [Apple](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/essex-votes/id6479920446)

### EV Connect

Pay for electronic vehicle charging at Town Hall online.

* [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/search?q=ev%20connect&c=apps&hl=en_US&gl=US)
* [Apple](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ev-connect/id1197712470)

### GovAlert (Report a Concern)

Use this online service to report service needed to the Township.

* [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/search?q=govalert&c=apps&hl=en_US&gl=US)
* [Apple](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/govalert/id1181460828)

### PSE&G

Pay utility bills and report power outages online.

* [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/search?q=pseg&c=apps&hl=en_US&gl=US)
* [Apple](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/pse-g/id1490690982)

### Recycle Coach

Discover local garbage and recycling disposal information for thousands of household items.

* [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/search?q=recycle%20coach&c=apps&hl=en_US&gl=US)
* [Apple](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/recycle-coach/id1071714967)
